## Background

Andre Piros is Kovac Defense Services' operational commander at the Garganta facility, serving as the on-the-ground enforcer of KDS authority and the direct subordinate of Anders Johanson. Where Johanson operates at the strategic level -- pitching services, negotiating contracts, designing systems -- Piros handles the execution: authorizing searches, managing security incidents, deflecting questions from facility staff, and ensuring KDS's classified programs remain compartmentalized.

Piros communicates in clipped, authoritative prose. His emails are short, directive-driven, and designed to end conversations rather than open them. When Dean Lockwood asks what the "Rise Project" is after receiving a shipment of HSUs designated for it, Piros responds: "If you haven't been informed, I assume you don't need to know. I suggest you speak to your superiors if you have questions." This is Piros in his element -- a gatekeeper who treats information as a controlled substance, dispensing it only on a need-to-know basis and redirecting queries upward or sideways, never answering them himself.

His most consequential documented action is authorizing the pursuit of "Patient Zero" -- the individual who escaped Garganta on December 28th, 2052, carrying what Piros describes as "extremely hazardous material." His search authorization to operative Hamsun is methodical and far-reaching: coastal patrols from Yucatan to Cancun, vessel searches along the Loop and Florida currents, full hazmat protocols, and contingency plans for stationing agents around the entire Gulf coast should the target reach Cuba. The target did escape the net, reaching Naples, Florida, and unleashing the NAM-V virus on the outside world. Piros's failure to contain this breach set in motion the global pandemic that killed billions.

Piros also served as the intermediary between Lockwood and Johanson. When Lockwood complained about inadequate security -- civilian breaches, violent incidents, unmanned checkpoints -- Piros forwarded the complaint to Johanson rather than resolving it himself, noting simply: "I have made Mr. Johanson aware of your concerns." When the execution of Igor Rostok by a KSO for entering a restricted area prompted outrage from Andrew Clinton, Piros's response was to cite the legal framework: the Santonian employee contract, Kovac's martial law provisions, and the national law permitting "punitive consequence." He expressed no regret and offered no concessions.

## Arc

- **2050-2051**: Piros is established as KDS's operational presence at Garganta, handling day-to-day security communications. Serves as the contact point Johanson directs facility staff toward.
- **July 2052**: Reports to Lockwood about an intercepted signal of unknown origin broadcasting from within the facility. Attaches an audio file and requests permission to investigate.
- **December 2052**: Authorizes an all-points search for a target who escaped the facility carrying hazardous material. Deploys extensive coastal patrols and warns that failure could compromise client privacy. The target escapes, becoming Patient Zero for the global NAM-V pandemic.
- **July 2053**: Defends the KSO execution of Igor Rostok by citing Kovac's martial law contract. Shows no acknowledgment that the response was disproportionate.
- **August 2053**: Receives Johanson's instruction to suppress the hydrostasis storage incident. Complies without question.
- **April 2054**: Directs Lockwood to route Rise Project HSU shipments to a private Kovac vault, forbidding any non-Kovac personnel from interacting with them. When Lockwood asks what the Rise Project is, Piros stonewalls him.

## Key Relationships

- **Anders Johanson (A029)** -- Piros's superior within KDS. Johanson sets strategy; Piros executes. Their communication is terse and hierarchical.
- **Dean Lockwood (A074)** -- Facility authority who frequently brings security concerns to Piros. The relationship is adversarial: Lockwood demands accountability while Piros deflects and compartmentalizes.
- **Andrew Clinton (B035)** -- SMC project manager who confronted Piros over Igor Rostok's execution. Piros responded with legal citations rather than empathy.
- **Janson Davies (A001)** -- Dreyfus executive whom Clinton CC'd on the Rostok complaint. Davies forwarded the matter to Piros, distancing himself from the incident.
- **Hamsun (A359)** -- KDS operative who received Piros's Patient Zero pursuit authorization.
