## Background

Andrew Clinton was the primary project manager for the Santonian Mining Company's operations at Garganta. He served as the critical link between Dreyfus Industries leadership (Janson Davies), the operational workforce, and the increasingly powerful Kovac Defense Services presence. Clinton was involved from the very beginning -- his earliest correspondence dates to April 2049, when he was cultivating contacts in the Yucatan to secure access to the Chicxulub site, and September 2049, when he reported the massive iridium deposit discovery to Davies: "If the density is consistent, we could be looking at upwards of 4,000 tons. A conservative estimate puts that at 300 billion at today's Iridium price."

Clinton's voice shifts dramatically depending on his audience. In all-staff emails, he deployed aggressive corporate optimism -- "From all of corporate at Santonian Mining, we want to congratulate you on an excellent first quarter working at the cutting edge of modern ore and mineral extraction!" -- while burying catastrophic news in the fine print. His infamous Spring Break email of April 2052 congratulated workers, cancelled all leave, and casually announced the NAM-V outbreak in the same breath, assuring staff that "the virus poses very little threat to you." In private correspondence, Clinton was far more candid and occasionally showed genuine warmth, referencing his daughter Ariana (whose 10th birthday was noted by Davies) and maintaining personal friendships with workers like Igor Rostok.

Clinton also installed the classified HearSay surveillance system throughout the facility, giving detailed schematics to his subordinate Artus Chaparon with strict instructions: "You are not aware of the Hearsay system, or its purpose." This dual nature -- the man who genuinely cared about individual workers while building the surveillance infrastructure that would be used against them -- defined his tenure at Garganta.

## Arc

**Pre-Construction (2049):** Contacts Gustavo Peres to secure Yucatan land access for SMC. Sends thinly veiled bribery correspondence referencing "donations to personal charity organizations." Reports the iridium vein discovery to Davies. Sends Garganta blueprints.

**Construction Overseer (2050-2051):** Manages facility construction. Oversees drilling operations starting July 2050. Deals with contractor logistics and the growing KDS presence.

**Narrative Controller (2052):** Installs the HearSay surveillance system (March). Issues the Spring Break all-staff email that buries the NAM-V outbreak (April). Enforces leave cancellations. Manages information flow between Dreyfus, SMC, and the workforce. Attempts to maintain morale through performative optimism while the situation deteriorates.

**Project Insight Transition (March 2052):** Davies congratulates Clinton on the launch of Project Insight but makes clear that mining operations are now subordinate: "where you drill, when you drill, what you drill, how you drill, and if you drill will now be dictated exclusively by the Project Insight team." Clinton's authority is effectively curtailed.

**Crisis of Conscience (mid-2053):** Igor Rostok, a Generalist Engineer and Clinton's personal friend, is shot and killed by a KSO for "entering a restricted area." Clinton is devastated and writes to Davies: "One of our employees has been killed for walking into the wrong area, and no one here seems to care... in what world is it okay to execute a person on the spot for trespassing? As I write that I realize how insane it is." Davies deflects, claiming Dreyfus has no involvement in Santonian policy. Andre Piros cites the SMC employee contract and martial law provisions.

**Death (October 13th, 2053):** According to the lore, Clinton was about to leave the facility to expose the truth about Garganta to the outside world. He was at the helipad when the catastrophic energy surge from the Inner -- triggered by Hammerstein's frequency experiment -- sent a quark-gluon plasma beam directly to his location, killing him instantly. The lore strongly implies this was not coincidental: the Collectors directed the blast to eliminate a threat to their long-term plan.

## Key Relationships

- **Janson Davies (A001)** -- Dreyfus Industries executive and Clinton's ultimate superior. Davies maintained careful plausible deniability in all correspondence while directing policy through "suggestions." The relationship was asymmetric: Clinton reported honestly; Davies managed optics.
- **Igor Rostok (C122)** -- Generalist Engineer and Clinton's personal friend. Rostok's execution by a KSO was the breaking point that drove Clinton to consider exposing the facility.
- **Andre Piros (A134)** -- KDS operational commander. Cold, legalistic, and unyielding when Clinton protested Rostok's killing. Cited contracts and martial law.
- **Gustavo Peres** -- Yucatan contact. Clinton bribed him through "charity donations" to secure SMC's access to the Chicxulub site.
- **Artus Chaparon (D017)** -- Subordinate tasked with installing the HearSay system. Clinton gave him tier 4 clearance access with strict secrecy requirements.
- **Ariana Clinton** -- His daughter, whose 10th birthday Davies referenced in correspondence. A rare glimpse of Clinton's personal life.
