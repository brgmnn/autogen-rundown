## Background

Dr. Rebecca Stokes arrived at Garganta from Houston in September 2054, joining Project Insight as the new analysis overseer. Her first task was methodically working through "The Hammerstein Collection" artifact by artifact, starting with the Matter Wave Projector. From her very first recording, Stokes demonstrated the qualities that defined her career at Garganta: meticulous scientific rigor, a willingness to confront established authority, and a growing unease about the people and systems surrounding her.

Stokes immediately established a practical research program for the Matter Wave Projector, coating it in silicon resin to prevent the "accidents" that had claimed two mining engineers who vanished upon contact. She noted with quiet alarm that the two witnesses (C067 and C085, one of whom was John Schaeffer) had personnel files marked that Kovac was "dealing with them." She sent material samples to her relative Dr. Howard Stokes for spectroscopic analysis off-site, circumventing the facility's information controls.

Before Hammerstein's death, Stokes was already his most vocal challenger. In a draft rebuttal that was never sent (the file ends mid-sentence with "You are a complete"), she confronted Hammerstein's refusal to consider that creatures might contain human DNA. Her argument was precise: the DNA was not merely "similar" to human -- it was human, at least in part. She proposed RNA editing as a mechanism, drawing parallels to Earth cephalopods, and suggested that some creatures might represent the end stage of NAM-V infection in humans. She even sarcastically proposed naming these specimens "Gallus Stokus."

## Arc

**Arrival and Analysis (September 2054):** Arrives from Houston. Begins systematic examination of the Hammerstein Collection. Initiates Matter Wave Projector research. Establishes off-site analysis channel through Howard Stokes.

**Scientific Challenger (late 2054):** Drafts confrontational rebuttal to Hammerstein's taxonomy, presenting evidence for human DNA in creatures and RNA editing as a mutation mechanism. The draft is never completed or sent.

**Inheriting Project Insight (June 2055):** Hammerstein, Connor, Briggs, and Gaubert are killed by creatures in the Intrepid Chamber. Stokes must write the company death announcement. Her draft reveals the emotional toll in real-time -- professional eulogy attempts disintegrate into ALL CAPS outbursts: "WHY WHY WHY," "FUCK ME," "SAY YOU'RE SORRY," and "I AM A LIAR I AM A LIAR I AM A LIAR." The mask finally drops entirely: "I am glad he's dead. I hated him, as did all of you... I hope those fucking creatures ripped the flesh from his bones while he screamed."

**Quantum Breakthrough (June 2055):** Analyzes the EF-01 expedition's final 5-millisecond data burst and discovers that the quantum frequency changed during translocation. Durant's team experienced time at 115x the rate of Garganta. This leads her to a revolutionary understanding of multi-dimensional physics: dimensions are differentiated by quantum frequency, superpositions can occur across dimensions, and the Matter Wave Projector exploits predictable cross-dimensional superpositions to "switch waves" and transport matter between realities.

**Paranoid Isolation (mid-2055):** Growing awareness of Kovac surveillance. Notes in one recording: "I can hear you out there." Fears that sharing her discovery with Hammerstein (before his death) would result in him taking credit, and that emailing Howard would expose her findings to monitoring systems. Increasingly isolated, talking only to the HearSay system she knows is being recorded.

## Key Relationships

- **Dr. Gallus Hammerstein (B014)** -- Her predecessor and antagonist. Stokes despised Hammerstein's narcissism and scientific rigidity. His death freed her professionally but left her writing a eulogy that exposed the depth of her loathing.
- **Dr. Howard Stokes (B341)** -- Off-site geologist, likely a sibling or relative. Stokes sent him material samples for spectroscopic analysis. She trusted him but feared their communications were monitored.
- **Nolan Connor (B229)** -- Colleague killed with Hammerstein. Stokes's draft reveals genuine grief: "I will personally miss Nolan the most."
- **Nadelle Gaubert** -- Researcher whose quantum entanglement work Stokes respected. Killed with Hammerstein.
- **Dr. James Durant (B255)** -- EF-01 expedition leader whose data burst provided the foundation for Stokes's quantum frequency discovery.
- **Frank Bishop** -- Stokes was present when Bishop returned alone through the Matter Wave Projector. She witnessed Andre Piros seize him before anyone could ask questions.
