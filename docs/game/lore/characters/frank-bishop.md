## Background

Frank Bishop was born in Lambeth, London, in 2001 to Sam and Evie Bishop. He joined the Santonian Strategic Investments Company (SSIC) as an intern in 2017 and worked for them for ten years. During his employment, Bishop installed spyware on multiple executive laptops and gathered sensitive corporate information -- specifically knowledge about Dreyfus Industries and the RX-Zika vaccination's long-term effects. He attempted to blackmail his superiors, and in retaliation, Janson Davies (Dreyfus Industries executive) had Bishop posted to Valencia, a city on the brink of civil war under Esparza's control, knowing that foreigners would almost certainly be killed.

Bishop survived three months as a hostage before being picked up. When he protested his innocence and demanded his records be checked, his interrogators revealed the full extent of Davies's retaliation: every trace of Frank Bishop had been erased. No birth records, no employment history, no visa paperwork. "Mr. Bishop, you don't exist. You never did." He was sent directly to the Legion program.

Even after twelve months of hydrostasis incubation (designated Genesis 1395), Bishop retained fragments of memory -- specifically the name Janson Davies and a murderous intent toward him. This extraordinary resistance to memory erasure marked him as unusual. He was given a Temporary class designation and subjected to further conditioning.

Bishop was later assigned to the EF-01 expedition through the Matter Wave Projector, where the team arrived on an alien jungle world and lost their connection to Garganta. Stranded for what felt like years (though far less time passed at Garganta due to temporal dilation), Bishop proved himself invaluable -- convincing the KSO operatives to help build outposts and infrastructure. Dr. Durant described him as "stoic" and irreplaceable. When the data cubes reactivated, Bishop touched them and vanished, teleporting back to Garganta. He was immediately seized by Andre Piros and KDS upon arrival, before any researchers could question him.

## Arc

**Pre-Garganta (pre-2050s):** Corporate employee at SSIC. Discovers sensitive information about Dreyfus Industries. Attempts blackmail. Gets erased and sent to die in Valencia.

**Capture and Conditioning:** Interrogated as prisoner. All identity records destroyed by Janson Davies. Sent to Legion program. Designated Genesis 1395. Undergoes twelve months of hydrostasis incubation but retains negative memories -- notably Davies's name. Receives additional conditioning cycles.

**EF-01 Expedition (~2055):** Deployed as KSO security detail for the EF-01 research team through the Matter Wave Projector. Stranded on an alien jungle world after the quantum tether to Garganta is severed. Takes a leadership role among the operatives, building outposts and ensuring the group's survival over the years that pass on the alien world.

**Return and Anomaly:** When data cubes reactivate, Bishop touches them and is instantly translocated back to Garganta. He appears in the Translocation Chamber with the Matter Wave Projector. He refuses or is unable to explain what happened. Andre Piros takes him into KDS custody immediately. Post-launch forensic analysis reveals a second security officer mask jammed under the launch platform, carrying Bishop's DNA but of a different design than his issued equipment. Bishop's original gear is all accounted for. This implies dimensional duplication -- two versions of Bishop existed simultaneously.

**Quad B452 (~2063):** Assigned by the Warden/BIOCOM system alongside Woods, Hackett, and Dauda. Status: Seek and Destroy. Deployed on expeditions into the Complex.

## Key Relationships

- **Janson Davies (A001)** -- The Dreyfus executive who erased Bishop's identity and sent him to die. Bishop's final coherent words during conditioning were a promise to kill him.
- **Dr. James Durant (B255)** -- EF-01 expedition leader. Relied heavily on Bishop for the group's survival. Described him as "stoic" and mourned his disappearance.
- **Andre Piros (A134)** -- KDS commander who seized Bishop upon his return from EF-01 and refused to provide further information to researchers.
- **Dr. Rebecca Stokes (B162)** -- Received Bishop upon his return to the Translocation Chamber. Noted his silence and Piros's interference.
- **Nolan Connor (B229)** -- Investigated the Bishop mask anomaly post-EF-01. Discovered the dimensional duplication evidence.
- **Woods (F-2056), Hackett (O-4711), Dauda (T-3701)** -- Fellow Quad B452 members.
