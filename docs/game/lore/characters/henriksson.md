## Background

Henriksson was a civilian specialist -- an engineer -- who survived the fall of Garganta by entering a Hydro-Stasis Unit (HSU). Like all personnel in the complex, he had been fitted with a neural control implant, making him subject to the Warden's commands upon awakening. He was one of the few non-military survivors preserved in the HSU network alongside thousands of KDS operatives.

Henriksson's story is told almost entirely through gameplay events and narrative context rather than D-Lock logs. No surviving documents bear his name as author or recipient. His existence is referenced in the base-game wiki, the lore video transcript, and the overarching narrative of Rundowns 7 and 8.

## Arc

**Liberation (Rundown 7, Original Worldline)**

In the original worldline, John Schaeffer -- the OG Schaeffer, who viewed the Warden as an enemy to be destroyed -- identified Henriksson in the HSU network and had the player squad free him. The players also removed Henriksson's control chip, liberating him from the Warden's influence. This made Henriksson one of only a handful of free-willed humans remaining in Garganta.

**Alliance with Schaeffer (Rundown 7)**

Henriksson worked alongside Schaeffer and the players to inject a virus into the Warden system. The goal was to seize control of BIOCOM from the Warden, giving Schaeffer and Henriksson command over the facility's systems. This plan succeeded -- Rundown 7 ended with the Warden compromised and Schaeffer in control of the original worldline's systems.

**Betrayal and Death (Rundown 8, Second Half)**

By the events of Rundown 8, Schaeffer's plan had escalated from escaping the complex to destroying it entirely via nuclear reactor meltdown. Henriksson did not want to die. He attempted to work with the player squad to break them free of their programming and betray Schaeffer -- to find a way out that did not involve mutual annihilation.

Schaeffer discovered the betrayal. In the mission following Henriksson's attempt to arrange a meeting with the players, they found his ID badge alongside a bloody pipe and a trail of blood leading away from the scene. No Sleeper carries a metal pipe. Schaeffer murdered Henriksson -- the engineer he had freed, the ally he had fought beside -- for the crime of wanting to survive.

## Key Relationships

- **John Schaeffer (C067):** Schaeffer freed Henriksson from his HSU, removed his control chip, and enlisted him as an ally. Later murdered him when Henriksson attempted to betray him.
- **The Player Squad (OG):** Henriksson tried to recruit the players to break free of Schaeffer's control. They found evidence of his murder in the next mission.
- **The Warden:** Henriksson was originally under the Warden's control via implant. His liberation was part of Schaeffer's rebellion against the Warden.
