## Background

Mr. Truth is the pseudonymous host of State of Truth AM, an independent shortwave radio broadcast operating outside the mainstream media ecosystem. A self-described truth-seeker and survivalist, he lived off-grid on a 40-acre compound in the high desert with his wife, Ramona (referred to on-air as "Mrs. Truth"), and their dogs. He began broadcasting well before the events at Garganta, positioning himself as a voice for "free-thinking minds" suspicious of government and corporate power.

Before his radio career, Mr. Truth worked a summer job at a lakeside diner near Lake Cachuma at age 17, an experience that profoundly shaped his worldview. The diner's owner, "Mr. Court," exploited the goodwill of young workers year after year, a formative lesson in recognizing how power operates through manufactured vulnerability. This parable became central to his broadcasting philosophy: teaching listeners to see through the masks of those who would exploit them.

Mr. Truth was among the first public voices to question the Merida earthquake of 2049, pointing out that a 9.8 magnitude event in a zone with minimal seismic activity was impossible by conventional geology. He connected the earthquake to the Santonian Mining Company's suspiciously rapid acquisition of the iridium-rich land, and later linked SMC to Kovac Defense Services and Dreyfus Industries. While his theories were dismissed as conspiracy, many of his core claims proved accurate: the earthquake was not natural, and something far more significant than mining was happening underground.

As the NAM-V pandemic spread, Mr. Truth shifted from investigator to survivalist broadcaster, providing census data, survival advice, and unflinching descriptions of societal collapse. He urged listeners to head for isolation, stock supplies, and abandon any hope that government institutions would save them. His broadcasts became increasingly personal and raw as the world died around him.

## Arc

**Pre-2049 -- Curious investigator.** Mr. Truth reports on anomalies: the Merida earthquake occurring in a seismically inactive zone, the suspicious timing of SMC's iridium discovery, and his own "false memories" where he clearly recalls the quake happening in March rather than April 2049. He asks listeners if they share this experience, hinting at timeline alterations tied to the Collectors' activities.

**~2052-2053 -- Pandemic chronicler.** As NAM-V devastates the United States, Mr. Truth obtains leaked CONDAM military statistics showing the US population dropping from 474 million to 302 million in just three years. He delivers hard numbers and actionable survival advice, urging listeners to isolate, arm themselves, and abandon faith in government solutions.

**~2053 -- Hunted fugitive.** FBI and Kovac Defense Services vehicles converge on his compound. Mr. Truth grabs his weapons, Mrs. Truth, and his dogs, and flees via back roads into the wilderness. He broadcasts from secret locations using burner phones, declaring himself "the cancer in their system."

**~2053-2054 -- On the road.** Mr. Truth and Mrs. Truth travel back roads avoiding population centers, witnessing mass graves, funeral pyres, bodies bulldozed into ditches, and executed civilians. His broadcasts become apocalyptic witness testimony, describing "the death rattle of our species."

**~2054 -- Castle Truth.** After 70 days in the wilderness, Mr. Truth resurfaces from a fortified secondary location he calls "Castle Truth." He is defiant, speaking of Babylon burning and listing the full scope of what has been revealed: aliens, the virus, clones, parasites, false memories.

**~2057 -- Grief in Pahrump.** In his final known broadcasts, Mr. Truth stands on the outskirts of Pahrump, Nevada, a ghost town. Mrs. Truth (Ramona) is dead. In a devastating slip, he refers to himself in the third person: "I am glad Mr. Truth is not here to see this." His voice breaks as he questions whether he was wrong about her, suggesting he may have driven her away or failed to protect her. The broadcast is intercepted by BIOCOM's shortwave monitoring system.

## Key Relationships

- **Mrs. Truth / Ramona** -- His wife. She held his hand through the early broadcasts, fled with him when KDS came, traveled the apocalyptic roads beside him, and died in or near Pahrump, Nevada. Her loss appears to break him entirely.
- **Truth Seekers** -- His audience and community. He addresses them as "my friends," urges them to maintain generators and communication lines, and views them as the last hope for humanity's survival.
- **Mr. Court** -- The Lake Cachuma diner owner from his youth. A parable figure representing those who exploit goodwill while wearing masks of humility.
- **Kovac Defense Services / FBI** -- His pursuers. KDS sent AT-36-B trucks alongside FBI SUVs to silence him, confirming his suspicions about government-corporate collusion.
- **Santonian Mining Company** -- The primary target of his early investigations. He correctly identified their suspicious acquisition of the Chicxulub site.
