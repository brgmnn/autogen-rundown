## Background

Nolan Connor is a specialist attached to Project Insight at the Garganta Complex, working in communications, telemetry, and forensic investigation. He operates in the space between the scientists conducting primary research and the security apparatus controlling access to it -- a position that gives him unique visibility into anomalies that others either miss or choose to ignore.

Connor's investigative instincts are sharp and methodical. When the EF-01 expedition through the Matter Wave Projector resulted in the loss of the entire team except KSO Bishop, Connor was the one who combed through the post-launch forensics. During the scrub-down of the translocation jump site, his team discovered a security officer mask jammed under the launch platform. The DNA matched Bishop -- the only operative who returned -- but Bishop already had all of his equipment, including his mask, and KDS confirmed he was using the same gear issued when he first arrived at Garganta. The mask was also a different design. This finding, which Connor meticulously documented in a report to Dr. Stokes, is one of the earliest pieces of evidence pointing to dimensional duplication: the mask belonged to an alternate-dimension Bishop.

Connor also recovered the fragmented recordings left by Dr. Durant in the Matter Wave Projector's memory bank. Working for several days to reassemble the corrupted data, he delivered two audio transcripts to Dr. Stokes -- the first confirming EF-01's arrival on an alien world, and the second documenting Bishop's disappearance via datacube years later. These recovered messages were the only evidence that Durant's team survived the translocation.

Beyond his forensic work, Connor was emotionally restrained but not detached. He presented evidence without editorializing, letting the facts speak for themselves. His writing style is clean, investigative, and professional, with just enough personal voice to show a man who cares about getting things right.

## Arc

**~2054 -- EF-01 forensics.** After Bishop's mysterious solo return from the EF-01 expedition, Connor conducts the post-launch forensic analysis. He discovers the anomalous mask under the translocation platform -- DNA-matched to Bishop but of a different design than Bishop's issued equipment. When he follows up with KDS operative Piros, he receives minimal cooperation: Piros confirms Bishop's equipment is accounted for, then simply stops responding. Connor secures the mask in the artifact vault and reports the finding to Dr. Stokes, acknowledging he is "stumped."

**~2054-2055 -- Data recovery.** Connor painstakingly reassembles fragmented recordings stored in the Matter Wave Projector's memory bank. He delivers two transcripts to Stokes: Durant's first field report from the alien jungle, and a later recording documenting Bishop's disappearance via the datacubes. Connor's cover notes are characteristically understated: "I know it's too little too late, but I thought you'd want to hear it."

**November 24, 2056 -- Final journal entry.** Connor records what may be his last message via HearSay. The world outside Garganta is dead. The board has forbidden anyone from leaving the complex. Some team members with families are begging to go, but no one is allowed out. Connor acknowledges the horror plainly: "We have destroyed the world, and there will be no one left to hold us accountable." He describes a sealed chamber deep in the Inner containing a biomass larger than anything previously encountered -- thousands of biosignatures and at least one massive unknown organism. The remaining survivors have avoided it for as long as possible, but there is no more time. Tomorrow, they will open the doors and face whatever is inside. Connor, who has spent his career working behind the scenes, insists on going with the combat team: "I refuse to die a cautious man. I choose to fight."

## Key Relationships

- **Dr. Rebecca Stokes (B162)** -- His direct supervisor and primary report recipient. Connor sends her the recovered EF-01 recordings and the anomalous mask findings. Their correspondence is professional and respectful.
- **Dr. James Durant (B255)** -- The EF-01 expedition leader whose fragmented recordings Connor recovered. Connor may have been the voice Alice heard calling for EF-01 across dimensional frequencies.
- **KSO Bishop** -- Subject of Connor's forensic investigation. The mask anomaly links Bishop to dimensional duplication.
- **Andre Piros (A134)** -- KDS operative who stonewalled Connor's investigation into Bishop's equipment, then took Bishop away before researchers could question him.
