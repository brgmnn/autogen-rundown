## Background

Vitori Molina was a contractor foreman at Garganta, responsible for overseeing construction and labor operations within the complex. His position placed him in direct, daily contact with the increasingly hostile security apparatus operated by Kovac Defense Services. As a foreman, Molina had the authority and visibility to notice patterns of behavior that individual workers might experience in isolation -- and the professional obligation to document what he saw.

For at least two months prior to April 2052, Molina had been filing formal reports documenting "the growing aggression towards Santonian employees by KSO representatives and Project Insight staff." Every one of those reports was destroyed.

## Arc

Molina's known log documents a single incident that encapsulates the worker experience at Garganta. On April 23rd, 2052, he returned to his sleeping quarters on Level 26 to find a KSO blocking entry. His belongings were being "processed" -- a euphemism for a search and data purge. When Molina attempted to enter his own quarters, the KSO physically restrained him, spraining his wrist and holding him face-down on the floor while a security detail ransacked his room.

When they finished, two months of correspondence -- emails and HearSay transcripts -- had been deleted. Every report Molina had filed about KDS aggression was gone, not just from his possession but from the system entirely. A HearSay microphone had been installed in his quarters.

The incident report Molina filed about this event reveals the institutional machinery of suppression. Rather than investigating the assault on a company foreman, the system scheduled Molina for a disciplinary hearing and suspended his socialization privileges. His personal statement was attached to the report but, as he noted, "I doubt if anyone even reads them anymore." The personal statement was subsequently removed from the file entirely: "[File removed]."

## Key Relationships

- **KDS/KSO Personnel:** Adversarial. Molina was physically assaulted and had his quarters searched by unnamed KSOs who refused to identify themselves.
- **Project Insight Staff:** Molina documented their growing hostility toward SMC workers alongside KDS aggression.
- **SMC Administration:** Filed reports through official channels, but his documentation was systematically erased.
