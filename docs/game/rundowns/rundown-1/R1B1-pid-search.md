# R1B1 - "PID Search"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 1.0: Deviation
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Storage (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Gather Items:** Search for 12 Personnel IDs across zones 39-45. Each zone contains up to 3 PIDs, for a total of 18 available. At most 5 zones need to be explored to collect all 12.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Scout

## Level Description

R1B1 is a collection-focused expedition where players must gather 12 Personnel IDs spread across multiple zones. The level branches into three distinct paths from the entry zone (Zone 38), each with different challenges:

- **Zone 41-42 Path:** Each zone contains a Scout. Zone 42 is pitch black and very small.
- **Zone 43-44-45 Path:** Features escalating alarm difficulty with increasing enemy spawns. Zone 45 contains significant fog requiring players to listen for sleepers.
- **Zone 39-40 Path:** Requires a Colored Key to access Zone 39. Contains a high concentration of Giants and Big Shooters. Zone 39A has fog covering the lower section.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Class II Alarm** in Zone 43
- **Class III Alarm** in Zone 44
- **Class III Alarm** in Zone 45
- **Colored Key** required for Zone 39 access
- **Fog** in Zone 45 (heavy) and Zone 39A (lower section)
- **Pitch black** Zone 42
- **Scouts** present in Zones 41 and 42
- **High Giant/Big Shooter density** in Zones 39-40
- Sentry gun placement recommended for alarm defense
