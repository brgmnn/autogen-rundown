# R1C2 - "Decode"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 1.0: Deviation
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Mines (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Gather Items:** Collect 10 Partial Decoders across multiple zones (2-3 per zone, approximately 14 total zones). Players navigate through Zones 92, 90, 93, 94, 96, 95, and either 98 or 99 before reaching the extraction point at Zone 100.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Scout
- Charger (introduced in this level - faster-moving, harder-hitting, and tougher than standard enemies)

## Level Description

R1C2 is a large collection mission spread across many zones in the Mines environment. This is the first expedition where the extraction point differs from the entry point, requiring forward progression to Zone 100 rather than backtracking. The extraction scan does not trigger any alarm.

The level introduces the Charger enemy type, which is faster-moving, harder-hitting, and tougher than standard enemies. Several zones feature Charger-exclusive alarm spawns, creating intense combat encounters. Players must use QUERY terminals to locate colored keys needed to unlock progression through Zones 96 and 100.

## Notable Mechanics

- **First level with separate extraction point** (Zone 100 instead of entry)
- **Extraction scan does not trigger any alarm**
- **Charger enemy type introduced** - faster, harder-hitting, tougher
- **Class III Alarm** in Zone 90
- **Class III Alarm** in Zone 94
- **Class III Alarm** in Zone 98 or 99 (Zone 99 spawns exclusively Chargers)
- **Class IV Alarm** in Zone 100 (spawns only Chargers)
- **Charger-exclusive alarm** in Zone 93
- **Colored Keys** required for Zones 96 and 100
- **QUERY terminal** in Zone 95 to locate key
- **Mines/C-Foam strategy:** Mines and sentries preferred over C-Foam for alarm defense
- **Charger stealth kills** require back attacks with full charge melee
