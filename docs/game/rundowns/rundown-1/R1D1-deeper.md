# R1D1 - "Deeper"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 1.0: Deviation
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Storage (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Reactor Shutdown:** Complete a reactor shutdown sequence across multiple zones. Navigate through zones 69-72, with optional resource gathering in zones 73-78, then extract.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Scout (multiple Scouts patrol the same zone)
- Shadow (nigh-invisible, slow-moving variant of the Striker; introduced in this level)

## Level Description

R1D1 is the final expedition in ALT://Rundown 1.0 and introduces both the Shadow enemy type and the Reactor Shutdown mission type. Shadows are nearly invisible, slow-moving variants of the Striker that create a unique stealth and awareness challenge.

The level requires navigating through heavily guarded zones with escalating alarm difficulties (Class IV and Class V) before reaching the reactor in Zone 72. Multiple Scouts patrol the same zones, requiring careful coordination and stealth. Zones 73-78 are optional resource zones with no alarms but contain Giants and Big Shooters.

During the reactor shutdown sequence, all lights turn off and enemy spawns consist solely of Shadows, creating an intense darkness-based combat encounter. After completing the shutdown, an Error Alarm spawns 3 Shadows approximately every 20 seconds. The team cannot access resources until completing at least one alarm.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Reactor Shutdown** mission type (introduced in this level)
- **Shadow enemy introduced** - nigh-invisible, slow-moving Striker variant
- **All lights turn off during reactor shutdown** with Shadow-only spawns
- **Multiple Scouts in the same zone** requiring coordinated stealth
- **Class IV Alarm** in Zone 69 (contains Scout)
- **Class IV Alarm** in Zone 72 (reactor location)
- **Class V Alarm** in Zone 70 (contains 1-2 Scouts)
- **Class V Alarm** in Zone 71
- **Zones 73-78:** No alarms, optional resource zones with Giants and Big Shooters
- **Error Alarm** after reactor shutdown (3 Shadows every ~20 seconds)
- **Cannot access resources** until at least one alarm is completed
- **Recommended loadout:** Bio Tracker; Burst or Sniper sentries
- **Melee Scout elimination** recommended for stealth zones
