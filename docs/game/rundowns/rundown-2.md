# ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection

## Overview

- **Release Date:** December 7, 2022
- **Tiers:** A, B, C, D, E
- **Total Expeditions:** 10

## Narrative Summary

Prisoners are tasked with locating and retrieving the Neonate HSU from cold storage for processing in the subsequent rundown. This is a re-release of the original Rundown 002 and marks the first appearance of the Infection mechanic. The original R2E1 holds the lowest completion rate in GTFO history at approximately 0.99-1.4%.

## New Features & Content

- **New Mechanic:** Infection
- **New Weapons:** Burst Rifle (Malatack CH 4), Machine Pistol (Raptus Treffen 2), Techman Arbalist V Machine Gun
- **Note:** Unlike the original R2, all ALT:// levels were accessible immediately without Warden Restrictions
