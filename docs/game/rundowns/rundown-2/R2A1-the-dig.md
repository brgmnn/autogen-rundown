# R2A1 - "The Dig"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Mines (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Retrieve Item: Use QUERY to locate 2 Cargo Crates. Enter Zone 103 and collect a Key to unlock Zone 106's door. Enter Zone 104 or 105 (CLASS II ALARM) to retrieve the first Cargo Crate. Enter Zone 106 (CLASS IV ALARM) and retrieve the second Cargo Crate. Return to the entry point and extract with both crates. Entering the extraction scan triggers an Error Alarm that spawns 4 Strikers every ~15 seconds.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

The Dig is the first expedition in ALT://Rundown 2.0 and serves as an introduction to several key mechanics that define the rundown. Players descend into the Mines environment and must retrieve two Cargo Crates from different zones. The level introduces the Retrieve Item mission type, where items must be physically carried by players, slowing movement and preventing most actions until dropped. The level also introduces Infectious Fog, which appears in minor patches in the final room and becomes a major threat in later levels.

The level structure involves using QUERY to locate the crates, then navigating through zones 102-106, dealing with alarm doors of varying difficulty. The extraction sequence is notably dangerous as it triggers an Error Alarm with continuous Striker spawns.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Retrieve Item mission type:** Items must be physically carried, slowing movement and preventing most actions until dropped
- **Infectious Fog:** Present in minor patches in the final room; introduces the mechanic for later levels
- **Error Alarm on extraction:** Triggers 4 Striker spawns every ~15 seconds during extraction scan
- **Alarm doors:** CLASS II ALARM (Zone 104/105), CLASS IV ALARM (Zone 106)
- **Zones:** 102, 103, 104, 105, 106 with varying resource distributions (ammo, medical, tools, disinfection)
