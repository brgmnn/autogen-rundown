# R2B1 - "Sacrifice"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Clear a Path: Navigate from one end to the other through multiple zones. Proceed through Zone 717 (featuring a Blood Door), optionally explore Zone 718 for extra resources, retrieve a colored key from Zones 720/721, then reach the Apex Door to Zone 719 and complete an extraction scan. The extraction scan spawns six active Giants.

### Secondary Objective

(Optional) Enter Zone 718 for extra resources [CLASS III ALARM]. Provides additional ammo, medical supplies, and tools at higher alarm risk.

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Scout
- Hybrid

## Level Description

Sacrifice is the first B-tier expedition and introduces the Blood Door mechanic -- a variant of Security Door that spawns awakened enemies behind it when opened. The level is a linear Clear a Path mission where players must navigate through the Mines to reach an extraction point.

The level is notable for featuring the first alarm class higher than five in the entire rundown, specifically a Class VII alarm at the Apex Door. The culmination of the level is a dangerous extraction event where entering Zone 719's extraction scan triggers the spawning of six active Giants, requiring careful resource management and coordination.

Players navigate through Zones 716-721, with Zone 718 serving as an optional high-risk/high-reward detour for extra supplies.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Blood Door introduction:** First appearance of Blood Doors, which spawn awakened enemies behind them when opened
- **Class VII Alarm:** First expedition to feature an alarm class higher than five, at the Apex Door
- **Extraction event:** Entering Zone 719's extraction scan triggers 6 active Giants
- **Optional resource zone:** Zone 718 behind CLASS III ALARM provides significant extra resources (220 ammo, 140 medical, 80 tools)
- **Zones:** 716, 717, 718, 719, 720, 721
