# R2B2 - "Power Corrupts"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Distribute Powercells: Locate and insert Power Cells into Generators across multiple zones. Use QUERY and PING to locate Generators. Insert Power Cells in Zone 265 (contains Giants and Big Shooters), Zone 266 (Strikers, Shooters, Spitters), Zone 267 (Strikers, Shooters), and Zone 268 or 269 (CLASS V ALARM, containing four Scouts each). Proceed to Zone 270 (Strikers, Shooters, Spitters) and extract from Zone 271 (CLASS V ALARM with BLOOD DOOR).

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

Power Corrupts introduces two significant new elements to the rundown: the Power Cell carry mechanic and the Spitter enemy type. Players must distribute Power Cells to Generators scattered across multiple zones in the Mines environment.

The Spitter is a notable new enemy -- an invincible, stationary creature that spreads Infection. It can only be temporarily disabled by shining a flashlight or other light source on it, making it a persistent environmental hazard rather than a traditional combat encounter.

The level progresses through zones with increasing difficulty, culminating in zones with CLASS V ALARM doors and Scout-heavy rooms. The extraction point in Zone 271 combines a CLASS V ALARM with a Blood Door, creating a particularly dangerous exit sequence.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Power Cell mechanic:** Introduces the carry-able Power Cell item as a core objective mechanic
- **Spitter enemy:** Invincible, stationary enemy that spreads Infection; only temporarily disabled by light
- **Alarm doors:** CLASS V ALARM doors in Zones 268, 269, and 271
- **Blood Door:** Present at the extraction point in Zone 271
- **Scout-heavy zones:** Zones 268 and 269 each contain four Scouts
- **Zones:** 265, 266, 267, 268, 269, 270, 271 with varying resource distributions; Zone 267 offers the most tools (80%) and medical supplies (120%)
