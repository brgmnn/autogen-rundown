# R2B3 - "Pathfinder"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Mines (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Establish Uplink: Introduces the Establish Uplink mission type where one player must input a series of specific passwords on a given terminal while the other players defend them against hordes of enemies. Use QUERY to locate the Uplink Terminal in Zone 166 or 167. Enter Zone 164 (Class IV Alarm) to collect colored keys unlocking access to Zones 166/167. Enter the determined zone (Class V Alarm) and complete the 5-code uplink. Completing the uplink triggers an Error Alarm spawning 3-4 Strikers every ~15 seconds for the remainder of the expedition. Retreat and extract.

## Level Description

Pathfinder is the fourth expedition in ALT://Rundown 2.0 and introduces the Establish Uplink mission type. This mission type requires team coordination: one player focuses on inputting terminal passwords while the remaining players defend against incoming enemy waves.

The level structure has players using QUERY to determine whether the uplink terminal is in Zone 166 or 167, then working through alarm doors to reach it. Zone 165 is an optional exploration area with high Giant concentration but extra resources. The 5-code uplink is the central challenge, and upon completion, an Error Alarm begins that persists for the rest of the mission, continuously spawning 3-4 Strikers every ~15 seconds.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Establish Uplink mission type:** One player inputs passwords on a terminal while others defend; first appearance in the rundown
