# R2B4 - "Septic"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Mines (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Retrieve Item: Use QUERY to locate a Fog Turbine in either Zone 764 or 765. Navigate through multiple zones collecting keys, enter the target zone containing heavy Spitter concentrations, retrieve the Fog Turbine, progress to Zone 763, and extract with the item. An Error Alarm triggers upon extraction scan initiation.

### Secondary Objective

None (though an optional secondary Fog Turbine is available to collect)

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Scout
- Spitter (large quantities, spreading Infection)

## Level Description

Septic is the fifth and final B-tier expedition in ALT://Rundown 2.0. The level heavily features Infectious Fog and Spitters, making infection management a central challenge. Players must retrieve a Fog Turbine from deep within the level, navigating through fog-filled and pitch-black zones with heavy Spitter presence.

The Infectious Fog mechanic reduces maximum player health proportionally to infection level, making disinfection resources critical. The Fog Turbine equipment eliminates fog in a sphere around the carrying player, serving as both the objective item and a crucial survival tool during the mission.

The level progresses through Zones 760-765, with the target zones (764/765) being particularly dangerous due to darkness and heavy Spitter concentrations.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Infectious Fog:** Pervasive throughout the level; reduces maximum player health proportionally to infection level
- **Fog Turbine:** Equipment that eliminates fog in a sphere around the carrying player; serves as both objective and survival tool
- **Pitch-black zones:** Zones 764/765 feature darkness combined with heavy Spitter presence
- **Error Alarm on extraction:** Triggers upon starting the extraction scan
- **Alarm doors:** Zone 764 (CLASS V), Zone 765 (CLASS IV), Zones 762-763 (CLASS IV)
- **Optional secondary Fog Turbine:** Available for additional collection
- **Zones:** 760, 761, 762, 763, 764, 765
