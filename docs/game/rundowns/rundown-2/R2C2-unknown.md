# R2C2 - "???"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Mines (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Gather Items: Collect 7 Personnel IDs across multiple zones. Retrieve a Power Cell from Zone 113, activate a Generator in Zone 112 to progress, navigate through fog-filled Zone 114, and search Zones 116/118/119 for additional Personnel IDs. Collecting the 7th Personnel ID spawns 6 Giants simultaneously. Extract after collecting all IDs.

### Secondary Objective

Retrieve a Fog Turbine from Zone 113 (optional but recommended for fog-filled zones)

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant (high concentration in Zones 116 and 119; 6 spawn upon collecting final ID)
- Big Shooter
- Hybrid (present in Zone 118)
- Spitter (Infectious Fog in Zone 114)

## Level Description

R2C2 features an unknown/redacted level name ("???") and presents one of the most challenging C-tier experiences. The core objective is collecting 7 Personnel IDs scattered across a sprawling zone layout while managing a persistent Error Alarm that runs from the very start of the expedition.

The ever-present Error Alarm spawns 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~45 seconds throughout the entire mission, representing a new implementation where the alarm persists from mission start rather than appearing only during escape sequences. This creates constant combat pressure as teams search for Personnel IDs.

Zone 114 contains Infectious Fog, Zone 115 has a Disinfection Station, and Zone 117 serves as a critical mid-mission resupply point. The climactic moment comes when collecting the 7th and final Personnel ID triggers 6 Giants to spawn simultaneously.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Persistent Error Alarm from start:** Spawns 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~45 seconds for the entire expedition, from mission start
- **Blood Doors:** Three zones (116/118/119) feature Blood Doors
- **Infectious Fog:** Zone 114 contains Infectious Fog
- **Disinfection Station:** Located in Zone 115
- **Giant spawn event:** Collecting the 7th Personnel ID spawns 6 Giants simultaneously
- **Power Cell puzzle:** Must retrieve Power Cell from Zone 113 to activate Generator in Zone 112
- **Alarm doors:** Zone 114 (CLASS III ALARM), Zone 115 (CLASS II ALARM)
- **No sleeping enemies:** First half of the level has no sleeping enemies
- **Zones:** 112, 113, 114, 115, 116, 117, 118, 119
- **Recommended loadout:** Mine Deployer for alarm containment; melee weapons during Error Alarms to conserve ammunition; avoid Spear due to charging mechanics
