# R2D1 - "Statistics"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Mines (Main sector)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Activate Generator Cluster: Retrieve five Power Cells from various zones throughout the level and insert them into a central Generator Cluster located in Zone 606. This is an inverse mechanic compared to the standard "Distribute Power Cells" objective, as cells must be collected from the environment and brought to a central location rather than distributed outward.

### Secondary Objective

Access Zone 615 (Disinfection Station) by obtaining a colored key from Zone 608

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

Statistics is the first D-tier expedition and features the most Infectious Fog in the entire rundown, yet notably lacks a Fog Turbine -- distinguishing it from other fog-heavy missions. The core objective inverts the standard Power Cell distribution mechanic: instead of distributing cells to generators, players must collect five cells from across the level and bring them to a central Generator Cluster in Zone 606.

The level spans 11 zones (605-615) with extensive fog coverage. As Power Cells are inserted into the Generator Cluster, the fog level progressively lowers, creating a satisfying feedback loop where progress directly improves visibility and safety. However, taking some infection is effectively required to insert Power Cells, making infection management critical.

The level contains 14 Scouts spread across multiple zones, the highest concentration in the rundown. Zone 611 alone contains 7 Scouts. The final Power Cell insertion triggers a CLASS V alarm that persists until extraction, and fog rises during this final alarm sequence. The extraction scan lasts 88 seconds with a full team.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Inverse Power Cell mechanic:** Collect cells from zones and bring to central Generator Cluster, rather than distributing outward
- **Progressive fog reduction:** Fog lowers as Power Cells are inserted into the Generator Cluster
- **No Fog Turbine:** Despite having the most infectious fog in the rundown, no Fog Turbine is available
- **Mandatory infection:** Taking some infection is effectively required to insert Power Cells
- **14 Scouts:** Highest Scout concentration in the rundown; Zone 611 alone has 7
- **Final alarm:** Last Power Cell insertion triggers a CLASS V alarm until extraction; fog rises during this sequence
- **88-second extraction scan** with full team
- **Blood Doors:** Present in Zones 610/612
- **Alarm doors:** Multiple CLASS IV and CLASS V alarms throughout
- **Colored keys:** Required for accessing certain zones
- **Zones:** 605, 606, 607, 608, 609, 610, 611, 612, 613, 614, 615
- **Known bugs:** Spitter Audio Bug causes audio degradation and crashes; first alarm frequently spawns stuck enemies. Modded fixes recommended.
