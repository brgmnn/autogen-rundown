# R2D2 - "Powerless"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Reactor Startup: Navigate through multiple zones to locate and input reactor verification codes. Some (sometimes all) codes for the reactor verification are not given upfront and must be retrieved from terminals distributed throughout the level. The verification timer is extended to accommodate code-gathering time. The reactor is located in Zone 192, with codes distributed across Zones 193-198. The reactor startup involves 10 phases with varying wave durations and enemy patterns.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Scout
- Hybrid

During reactor waves, only Strikers, Shooters, Giants, and Hybrids spawn, with Giant/Hybrid frequency increasing across later waves.

## Level Description

Powerless is the ninth expedition overall and the second D-tier level. It introduces the Reactor Startup mission type, which involves locating and inputting verification codes into a reactor terminal while defending against waves of enemies. The twist in this level is that some or all of the verification codes are not provided upfront -- players must search terminals throughout the level to find them, adding an exploration/puzzle element to the wave defense format.

The reactor startup consists of 10 phases with escalating difficulty. Enemy composition during reactor waves is limited to Strikers, Shooters, Giants, and Hybrids, with the proportion of Giants and Hybrids increasing in later phases.

After completing the reactor startup, an Error Alarm triggers that spawns 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~20 seconds until extraction, creating a tense escape sequence.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Reactor Startup mission type:** 10-phase wave defense with verification code input
- **Missing verification codes:** Some or all codes must be retrieved from terminals throughout the level rather than being given upfront
- **Extended verification timer:** Timer extended to allow code-gathering between waves
- **Escalating reactor waves:** 10 phases with increasing Giant/Hybrid frequency
- **Post-reactor Error Alarm:** Spawns 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~20 seconds until extraction
- **Dense fog:** Zone 198 contains dense fog layer
- **Fog Turbine:** Available in Zone 197
- **Alarm doors:** CLASS IV and CLASS V alarms in specific zones
- **Zones:** 190, 191, 192, 193, 194, 195, 196, 197, 198, 199, 200
