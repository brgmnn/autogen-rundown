- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 2.0: Infection

## Level Description

Crib is the final expedition of ALT://Rundown 2.0 and the first E-tier expedition in GTFO history. It is considered extremely challenging and features nearly all game mechanics in one level. The objective is to retrieve and extract with the Neonate HSU, a special biological specimen.

The level is a gauntlet through Zones 596-607, combining large quantities of Infectious Fog, continuous enemy waves, environmental hazards, and nearly every enemy type in the game. It introduces the Big Shadow enemy variant, an upgraded version of the Shadow that poses an even greater threat in dark conditions.

The level throws everything at the players: Surge Alarms spawning continuous enemy waves, a scaling Error Alarm that continuously spawns Strikers for the remainder of the expedition, rolling blackouts that temporarily disable lights, Blood Doors spawning Shadow-class enemies, and multiple Power Cell puzzles to unlock progression.

## Notable Mechanics

- **First E-tier expedition:** The first E-tier level in GTFO, representing maximum difficulty
- **Neonate HSU:** Special biological specimen that must be carried to extraction
- **Big Shadow introduction:** First appearance of the Big Shadow enemy variant
- **Cluster Scans:** First expedition featuring the Cluster Scan alarm type
- **Surge Alarms:** Continuous enemy wave spawning (Class III and IV variants)
- **Scaling Error Alarm:** Continuously spawns Strikers for the remainder of the expedition
- **Rolling blackouts:** Lights temporarily disabled, creating vulnerability to Shadow enemies
- **Blood Doors:** Spawn Shadow-class enemies when opened
- **Large quantities of Infectious Fog:** Pervasive throughout the level
- **Fog Turbine and Fog Repeller management:** Both tools critical for survival
- **Multiple Power Cell puzzles:** Required to unlock progression through zones
- **Nearly all game mechanics:** Combines almost every mechanic introduced in the rundown into a single level
- **Zones:** 596, 597, 598, 599, 600, 601, 602, 603, 604, 605, 606, 607
