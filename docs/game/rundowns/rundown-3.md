# ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel

## Overview

- **Release Date:** March 2, 2023
- **Tiers:** A, B, C, D
- **Total Expeditions:** 7
- **Location:** Labs sector

## Narrative Summary

The narrative follows the Neonate HSU through stages of resuscitation, purification, and neural imprinting. Prisoners work through power systems and biological preparation before a final neural frame imprinting sequence. R3C1 remakes the original Rundown Alpha level. The rundown is noted as being easier than R2, likely in response to the impressively low completion rates of that rundown.

## New Features & Content

- **First Boss:** Mother (B-tier)
- **Final Boss:** Big Mother (D-tier)
- **Restored Weapons:** HEL Gun, HEL Rifle, Rifle, Burst Cannon
