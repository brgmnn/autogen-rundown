# R3A1 - "Resuscitation"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Research
- **Zones:** 6 (Zone 250-255)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Retrieve the Neonate HSU, transport it to Zone 251, locate a Key in either Zone 252 or 253, then proceed to Zone 255 to insert the Neonate into a Neurogenic Cardiac Resuscitator unit. After processing, return the item to extraction.

This level introduces the Process Item objective type to the rundown.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

- Giant

- Hybrid

## Level Description

R3A1 is the first expedition of ALT://Rundown 3.0, serving as an introductory level that teaches players the Process Item objective mechanic. Players must navigate through six zones, retrieving a Neonate HSU and processing it through a Neurogenic Cardiac Resuscitator before extracting.

Zones 252 and 253 each contain a Scout along with a large concentration of Giants and Big Shooters, requiring careful stealth or coordinated combat. The key needed for progression is located in one of these two zones.

Processing the Neonate HSU in Zone 255 triggers an Error Alarm that spawns 2 Hybrids immediately, then 2 Strikers/Shooters approximately every 20 seconds. Players must manage these waves while completing the process and returning to extraction.

## Notable Mechanics

- **CLASS II ALARM** on doors to Zone 252/253
- **CLASS IV ALARM** on the door to Zone 255
- **Error Alarm** triggered by processing the Neonate HSU: spawns 2 Hybrids immediately, then 2 Strikers/Shooters every ~20 seconds
- Each of Zones 252 and 253 contains a Scout with heavy enemy presence (Giants, Big Shooters)
