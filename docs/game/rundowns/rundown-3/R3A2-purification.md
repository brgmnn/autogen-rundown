# R3A2 - "Purification"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Zones:** 5 (Zone 124-128)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Activate Generator Cluster:** Retrieve power cells from three separate zones (126, 127, 128) and insert them into a generator cluster in Zone 125. Each zone containing a power cell is locked behind a blood door. Fog reduces with each power cell insertion. The final power cell insertion triggers an error alarm spawning Strikers approximately every 10 seconds. Extract at the entry point in Zone 124.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

R3A2 serves as an easier version of ALT://R2D1 and functions as an excellent tutorial mission for the Activate Generator Cluster objective type. Players navigate through a mine environment with infectious fog as a persistent hazard.

Zone 124 is notably filled with infectious fog and exclusively populated with Giants, presenting a dangerous starting area. The three power cell zones (126, 127, 128) are each locked behind blood doors branching from Zone 125 where the generator cluster is located.

A key mechanic is the fog reduction that occurs with each power cell insertion, gradually improving visibility and reducing the infection hazard as players progress. However, the final power cell insertion triggers an error alarm that spawns Strikers approximately every 10 seconds, creating an intense extraction sequence.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Blood Doors** on Zones 126, 127, and 128
- **Infectious Fog** throughout, especially dense in Zone 124
- **Fog reduction** mechanic tied to objective progression -- inserting each power cell reduces fog levels
- **Error Alarm** triggered by final power cell insertion, spawning Strikers every ~10 seconds
- Zone 124 has concentrated Giant population within fog
