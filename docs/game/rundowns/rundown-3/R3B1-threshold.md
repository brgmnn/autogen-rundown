# R3B1 - "Threshold"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Establish Uplink:** Navigate through multiple alarm-gated zones to reach Zones 235 and 236, each containing a 4-CODE UPLINK terminal. Both uplink terminals must be established. Completing both uplinks triggers an Error Alarm that spawns 4 Strikers/Shooters approximately every 45 seconds for the remainder of the expedition. Extract through Zone 234.

## Level Description

R3B1 is a B-tier expedition focused on the Establish Uplink objective type. The level is structured around a series of progressively difficult alarm doors that gate access to two uplink terminals. Careful sequencing of objectives is critical to success.

The recommended approach is:

1. Enter Zone 232
2. Enter Zone 233 (triggers CLASS IV ALARM)
3. Open the door to Zone 234 (triggers CLASS V ALARM) -- this is recommended before completing both uplinks, as extraction is behind this door and the alarm becomes significantly harder after uplink activation
4. Enter Zones 235/236 (CLASS III ALARM) and establish the 4-CODE UPLINK terminals
5. Extract through Zone 234

The strategic decision of when to open the Zone 234 extraction door is the key challenge. Opening it before the uplinks are complete allows for a cleaner extraction path, since the Error Alarm after uplink completion makes all subsequent alarms much more dangerous.

## Notable Mechanics

- **CLASS III ALARM** on doors to Zones 235/236
- **CLASS IV ALARM** on the door to Zone 233
- **CLASS V ALARM** on the door to Zone 234 (extraction route)
- **Error Alarm** triggered after both uplinks complete: spawns 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~45 seconds permanently
- **4-CODE UPLINK** terminals in both Zone 235 and Zone 236
- Strategic door sequencing required -- opening extraction door before uplink completion is strongly advised
