# R3B2 - "Pressure"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Zones:** 7 (Zone 506-512)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Retrieve a Fog Turbine (optional), acquire Power Cells to unlock security doors, navigate through multiple zones, and ultimately insert a Neonate HSU into a Hydrostasis Depressurizer before extracting the processed item.

### Secondary Objective

None

None

## Level Description

R3B2 is a B-tier expedition that introduces two new enemy types to the game: the Baby Striker and the Mother (a mini-boss). The level revolves around a Process Item objective with significant tactical flexibility in routing.

Players must collect Power Cells to unlock security doors, with the choice between Zone 507 or 508 for initial acquisition. Zone 508 contains 2 Power Cells but has no resources and a high concentration of Giants and Big Shooters. Zone 511 provides extra resources or additional Power Cells as an optional detour.

The Mother boss encounter in Zone 510 is a significant event. Defeating the Mother while it is still sleeping/dormant is heavily advised, as an active Mother presents a far greater threat. The level culminates with inserting the Neonate HSU into the Hydrostasis Depressurizer in Zone 512, which triggers an Error Alarm that persists until extraction.

Infectious fog is present as an environmental hazard throughout parts of the level.

## Notable Mechanics

- **CLASS III ALARM** on Zone 507
- **CLASS IV ALARM** on Zone 510 (contains the Mother boss)
- **CLASS V ALARM** on Zone 508 (high enemy density, no resources)
- **CLASS IV ALARM with BLOOD DOOR** on Zone 512, triggers Error Alarm spawning enemies every ~20 seconds
- **Mother** mini-boss encounter in Zone 510 -- killing while dormant is strongly recommended
- **Baby Striker** enemies introduced for the first time
- **Infectious Fog** as environmental hazard
- **Fog Turbine** available as optional pickup
- Multiple routing options through zones provide tactical variety

## Resource Distribution

Zone 511 offers the highest resource concentration (160 ammo, 120 medic, 80 tool), making it a valuable optional zone. Zone 508 contains no resources despite having Power Cells, creating a risk/reward tradeoff.
