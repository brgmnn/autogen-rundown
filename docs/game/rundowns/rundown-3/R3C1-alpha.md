# R3C1 - "Alpha"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Zones:** 20 (Zone 1-20)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Retrieve HSU:** Locate and retrieve a Health Status Unit through a multi-stage operation. Query two zones to determine HSU location, collect colored keys from intermediate zones, navigate through infectious fog using fog repellers, and ultimately scan and extract the HSU while managing escalating alarm threats.

### Secondary Objective

None

None

## Level Description

R3C1 is a remake of "C1: Alpha" from Rundown Alpha, updated for ALT://Rundown 3.0. It is the single C-tier expedition and represents a significant difficulty increase over the B-tier levels.

The level spans 20 zones across a mine environment with infectious fog as a major hazard. Players must first query terminals to determine where the HSU is located, then collect colored keys from intermediate zones to access the HSU's location. Fog repellers are required to traverse the fog-filled Zone 13.

A notable feature is that Zones 15-20 contain only Chargers and Scouts, creating a distinct gameplay feel in the latter half of the expedition. Upon entering Zones 15 or 18, a "Mother scream" event occurs without actually waking enemies, serving as an atmospheric moment.

The retrieval of the HSU triggers an aggressive Error Alarm that spawns 3 Chargers every 20 seconds, with an additional Charger added each minute, creating an exponentially more dangerous extraction sequence.

The objective text contains a ROT47-encoded message: "Unexpected geographical data mismatch, area deemed unstable."

## Notable Mechanics

- **CLASS IV ALARM** on Zone 13
- **CLASS V ALARM** on Zones 15 and 18
- **Error Alarm** after HSU retrieval: spawns 3 Chargers every 20 seconds, +1 Charger per minute
- **Infectious Fog** requiring fog repellers for Zone 13 traversal
- **Mother scream** event upon entering Zones 15/18 (does not wake enemies)
- **Optional Zone 14** contains sleeping enemies and Scouts
- Zones 15-20 exclusively populated with Chargers and Scouts
- C-Foam Launcher is notably ineffective for this expedition
- Strategic door placement and mine deployment recommended for alarm management
- ROT47-encoded lore text in objective description
