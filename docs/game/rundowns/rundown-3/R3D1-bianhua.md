# R3D1 - "Bianhua"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 3.0: The Vessel
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Research
- **Zones:** Multiple (Zone 389-397)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Retrieve the Neonate HSU and transport it to Zone 397's nFrame to deactivate dual Error Alarms that begin immediately at the start of the expedition. Then navigate through additional zones to extraction. The level splits into two distinct phases: an initial combat-heavy rush to process the HSU, followed by a stealth-intensive push toward extraction.

**Starting Error Alarms (active from drop):**

- 4 Strikers Error: spawns ~6-7 seconds after drop-off, waves every ~45 seconds
- 1 Giant Error: spawns ~30 seconds after drop-off, waves every ~45 seconds

## Level Description

R3D1 "Bianhua" is the final mission of ALT://Rundown 3.0 and is widely regarded as one of the best missions in the game. The name references Daoist and Chinese Buddhist concepts of metamorphosis and reincarnation, connecting thematically to the Neonate HSU's transformation and the Sleepers storyline.

The expedition begins under immediate pressure with dual Error Alarms active from the start, forcing players into a combat-heavy rush phase. The key progression steps are:

1. Collect a Colored Key from either Zone 389 or 390 (pitch-black CLASS III ALARM zones)
2. Traverse Zone 391 (BLOOD DOOR) to reach Zone 396 (CLASS VI ALARM, fog-filled)
3. Insert the Neonate HSU into the nFrame at Zone 397, deactivating the Error Alarms but spawning an active Mother in the preceding zone
4. Optionally retrieve a Fog Turbine from Zone 397
5. Navigate Zone 392 (CLASS V ALARM) with sleeping Chargers and Big Chargers requiring careful stealth
6. Pass through Zone 393 (resource zone with Disinfection Station)
7. Activate the alarm at Zone 394's door (CLASS VI ALARM), which spawns a pMother (Big Mother)
8. Extract through Zone 395 (BLOOD DOOR requiring full team scan), which spawns massive amounts of active Strikers/Shooters (notably excluding Hybrids)

The level effectively tests players against the comprehensive enemy roster while combining both combat and stealth mechanics in distinct phases.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Dual Error Alarms** active from the very start of the expedition
- **CLASS III ALARM** on Zones 389/390 (pitch-black)
- **CLASS V ALARM** on Zone 392 (sleeping Chargers/Big Chargers)
- **CLASS VI ALARM** on Zone 396 (fog-filled) and Zone 394 (spawns pMother)
- **BLOOD DOORS** on Zone 391 and Zone 395 (extraction, requires full team scan)
- **Infectious Fog** in Zone 396
- **Mother spawn** when inserting HSU into nFrame
- **pMother (Big Mother) spawn** at Zone 394 alarm
- **Sleeping enemy navigation** in Zone 392 (Chargers, Big Chargers)
- Zone 395 spawns massive active Striker/Shooter waves but excludes Hybrids

## Resource Distribution

Zone 391 offers substantial supplies (300 ammo, 240 medical, 140 tools) while combat zones like Zone 394 provide minimal resources. Zone 393 serves as the primary resupply point with a Disinfection Station.
