# ALT://Rundown 4.0: Contact

## Overview

- **Release Date:** April 27, 2023
- **Tiers:** A, B, C, D, E
- **Total Expeditions:** 12

## Narrative Summary

This rundown focuses on deepening access through various sectors. The narrative involves collecting data drives (HDDs), activating cryogenic cases, establishing power grids, and confronting hostile entities. Players progress through power management challenges and climate stabilization issues, ultimately facing several hostile biomasses before advancing to the next quadrant.

## New Features & Content

- **First rundown featuring optional sectors in every mission** (Secondary and Overload objectives)
- **Restored Weapons:** HEL Autopistol, Auto Sentry (both buffed with HEL properties)
- **Lore:** First instance of character "Schaeffer" via logs
