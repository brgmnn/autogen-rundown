# R4A1 - "Cytology"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 4.0: Contact
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Gather Items (HDDs): Navigate to Zone 33 and search for HDDs. Proceed to Zone 36 (CLASS II ALARM) to collect a Bulkhead Key and additional HDDs. Use the key and controller in Zone 33 to access Zone 34 (CLASS II ALARM), then advance to Zone 35 (CLASS III ALARM). Collect 6 HDDs total from Zones 33-36 (8 available), then extract.

### Secondary Objective

Establish Uplink: Use a Bulkhead Key and the Bulkhead Door Controller in Zone 33 to enter Zone 61 (CLASS III ALARM). Collect 2 Power Cells in Zone 61 and insert them into the Generators there to unlock the Security Doors to Zones 62 and 63 (CLASS III CLUSTER ALARM). Enter Zones 62 and 63 and establish uplinks with the required Terminals (3-CODE UPLINK).

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

R4A1 is the opening expedition of ALT://Rundown 4.0. The name "Cytology" refers to the examination of a small number of cells for a diagnosis, including amongst other conditions, infectious organisms. The level introduces the Sectors mechanic -- optional objectives that provide new angles and extra challenge to expeditions.

Players start by navigating to Zone 33 to gather HDDs, using Bulkhead Keys to progressively unlock deeper zones. The main path moves through Zones 32-36, with escalating alarm classes gating progress. The secondary sector (Zones 61-63) provides an independent optional challenge involving Power Cell distribution and terminal uplinks.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Sectors:** This level introduces the concept of sectors -- optional objectives that are not required for expedition completion but provide additional challenge.
- **Cluster Alarm:** The secondary sector introduces Cluster Alarms, which create (usually) twice as many scans as a standard alarm scan, but which take very little time to complete.
- **Alarm Doors:** CLASS II and CLASS III alarms gate progression in the main sector; CLASS III and CLASS III CLUSTER alarms in the secondary sector.
- **Bulkhead Keys & Controllers:** Required to unlock paths between main and secondary sectors.
- **Power Cells & Generators:** Used in the secondary sector to unlock Security Doors.
- **Resource Distribution:** All zones provide 40% Ammo, 60% Medi, 40% Tool.
