# R4A3 - "Onwards"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 4.0: Contact
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Clear a Path: Enter Zone 28 and collect a Bulkhead Key (zone contains a Scout). Use the Bulkhead Key and Door Controller in Zone 27 to unlock Zone 29 (CLASS III ALARM). Collect another Bulkhead Key in Zone 29. Optionally enter Zone 31 for extra resources. Use the Bulkhead Key and Door Controller in Zone 29 to unlock Zone 30 (CLASS IV ALARM). Enter Zone 30 and extract.

### Secondary Objective

Gather Items (OSIP Hormones): Use the Bulkhead Key and Door Controller in Zone 27 to unlock Zone 145. Collect a Bulkhead Key and two colored Keys in Zone 145 (contains three Scouts). Use colored Keys to unlock Zones 146/147 (CLASS IV ALARM, each contains one Scout). Collect OSIP Hormones (up to 3 per zone, maximum 7 total) until reaching 5. Return to Main objective to complete expedition.

### Overload Objective

Terminal Command: Use the Bulkhead Key and Door Controller in Zone 29 to unlock Zone 195 (triggers Error Alarm spawning 3 Striker/Shooter enemies every ~50 seconds for the remainder of the expedition). Enter Zone 195 (contains two Scouts) and proceed to Zone 196 (CLASS IV ALARM, BLOOD DOOR). Enter Zone 197 and execute command `FLOODGATE_OPEN_S156F` (spawns 3 Hybrids and 3 Giants). Collect Bulkhead Key in Zone 197. Return to Main objective to complete expedition.

## Enemy Types

- Striker

## Level Description

R4A3 is the final A-tier expedition. The title "Onwards" derives from the slogan "Onwards and downwards!" attributed to the Santonian Mining Company, referenced in two in-game logs. The level is characterized by very scarce resources throughout the Main Sector, at least until reaching the final zones. Zone 31 serves as an optional resource cache.

The expedition features all three sectors (Main, Secondary, Overload) with escalating difficulty across each path. The Overload sector introduces persistent enemy spawning via Error Alarm and culminates in a terminal command that triggers a dangerous enemy wave.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Resource Scarcity:** Very little resources throughout the Main Sector until final zones. Zone 31 is an optional resource room (160 Ammo, 200 Medi, 80 Tool).
- **Blood Door:** Zone 196 features a Blood Door mechanic.
- **Error Alarm:** Opening the Overload Bulkhead Door spawns 3 Striker/Shooter enemies every ~50 seconds for the remainder of the expedition.
- **Alarm Doors:** CLASS III and CLASS IV alarms gate progression throughout all sectors.
- **Prisoner Efficiency:** Recommended to complete the Secondary objective before the Overload objective.
- **Resource Distribution:**
  - Main: Zone 27 (40A/0M/20T), Zone 28 (20A/40M/0T), Zones 29-30 (0/0/0), Zone 31 (160A/200M/80T)
  - Secondary: Zone 145 (60A/60M/40T), Zone 146 (40A/20M/20T), Zone 147 (40A/60M/40T)
  - Overload: Zone 195 (40A/80M/20T), Zone 196 (80A/80M/40T), Zone 197 (40A/60M/0T)
