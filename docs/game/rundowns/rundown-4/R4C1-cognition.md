# R4C1 - "Cognition"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 4.0: Contact
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Process Item (Datasphere): Collect a Colored Key and Power Cell from Zone 72. Use the Colored Key to unlock Zone 74 (CLASS IV ALARM). Enter Zone 74 and access one of three zones (75/76/77) containing a Charger and Blood Door to retrieve the Bulkhead Key. Insert the Power Cell into a Generator in Zone 72 to unlock Zone 73 (CLASS V ALARM). Enter Zone 73 with the Datasphere. Use the Bulkhead Key with the Bulkhead Door Controller in Zone 73 to access Zone 78. Insert the Datasphere into the NFrame in Zone 78 (triggers an Error Alarm spawning 2 Hybrids initially, then 2 Strikers/Shooters every ~10 seconds). Return to Zone 73 and proceed to Zone 79 (CLASS III ALARM containing a Tank boss). Extract from Zone 80 with the Datasphere.

### Secondary Objective

Establish Uplink: Use the Bulkhead Key to unlock the Bulkhead Door to Zone 311 from Zone 73 (CLASS III ALARM spawning Chargers). Locate and execute a 12-CODE UPLINK at the Uplink Terminal in Zone 311 (spawns additional Chargers alongside regular enemies; yields another Bulkhead Key upon completion). Return to the main objective to complete the expedition. Notable for having an extremely long Terminal Uplink compared to previous expeditions.

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

R4C1 is the seventh expedition in the rundown and introduces two major new enemy types. The Tank is described as "a combat powerhouse which can easily wipe teams single-handedly if they're not prepared for it." Unlike the Mother boss (which primarily spawns Baby Strikers for defense), the Tank is a direct combat threat. Like Mother, it possesses a weakness to rear attacks -- spreading out and surrounding it gives the team much better odds.

The Charger enemy type also debuts here, appearing in both the Main and Secondary sectors. The Secondary sector is notable for its extremely long 12-CODE UPLINK, significantly longer than any previous expedition's uplink.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Tank Boss (New):** First appearance of the Tank mini-boss in Zone 79. A direct combat powerhouse weak to attacks from behind. Requires tactical positioning -- spreading out and surrounding it is recommended.
- **Charger Enemy (New):** First appearance of Charger enemies, found in Blood Door zones and spawned during Secondary alarm.
- **Datasphere & NFrame:** The Main objective revolves around processing a Datasphere through an NFrame, triggering an Error Alarm upon insertion.
- **Error Alarm:** Inserting the Datasphere spawns 2 Hybrids initially, then 2 Strikers/Shooters every ~10 seconds.
- **Blood Doors:** Zones 75, 76, and 77 feature Blood Doors requiring selection of one zone path.
- **12-CODE UPLINK:** The Secondary sector features an extremely long uplink compared to previous expeditions.
- **Alarm Doors:** CLASS IV (Zone 74), CLASS V (Zone 73), CLASS III (Zones 79, 311).
- **Resource Distribution:** Zone 311 (Secondary) provides significantly higher resources (140% Ammo, 180% Medi, 80% Tool) compared to main zones.
