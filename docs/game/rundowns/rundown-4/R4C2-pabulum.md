# R4C2 - "Pabulum"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 4.0: Contact
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Gather Items (GLP-1 Canisters): Collect 15 GLP-1 Canisters in Zones 8-15 (20 total available). Navigate to Zone 10, optionally enter Zones 13, 12, and 11. Use a Colored Key to access Zone 8, proceed through Zones 9, 14, and 15. Extract at Zone 7 entry point once 15 canisters are collected. Zones 8 and 9 contain large Giant concentrations.

### Secondary Objective

Gather Items (Personnel IDs): Use Bulkhead Key and Door Controller in Zone 11 to access Zone 53. Collect another Bulkhead Key there. Progress to Zone 54 with Surge Alarm, use QUERY command to locate 2 Personnel IDs in Zones 55/56. Retrieve them and return to main area.

### Overload Objective

Retrieve Item (Datasphere): Use Bulkhead Key and Door Controller in Zone 54 to open passage to Zone 241. Traverse Zone 241 to locate a Datasphere and transport it to Zone 7 entry point. Picking up the Datasphere triggers an Error Alarm spawning 6 Shadows every ~10 seconds.

## Level Description

R4C2 is the eighth expedition and takes place in the Mines environment. It introduces the Shadow Scout variant, which is identical in every aspect to the regular Scout but is entirely invisible except for its tentacles. The level features a wide variety of enemy types across its three sectors, with the Overload sector being particularly dangerous due to Shadows, Big Shadows, and three Shadow Scouts in Zone 241.

The Main sector requires collecting a large number of GLP-1 Canisters spread across many zones, while the Secondary and Overload sectors progressively introduce more dangerous Shadow-type enemies.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Shadow Scout (New):** Identical to regular Scouts but entirely invisible except for tentacles. Appears in Secondary and Overload sectors.
- **Big Shadow:** Larger Shadow variant appearing in the Overload sector.
- **Surge Alarm:** Reappears for the first time since R2E1, used in Secondary and Overload sectors.
- **Error Alarm:** Picking up the Datasphere in the Overload triggers spawning of 6 Shadows every ~10 seconds.
- **Infectious Fog:** Present in Zones 14-15 and secondary areas.
- **Spitter Infection:** Present in the Overload sector.
- **Fog Turbine:** Optionally collectible in Zone 11.
- **QUERY Command:** Used in the Secondary sector to locate Personnel IDs.
- **Prisoner Efficiency:** Recommended completion order is Secondary and Main objectives before Overload.
- **Resource Distribution:** Zone 241 (Overload) provides the highest resources (160 Ammo, 140 Medi, 120 Tool).
