# R4D2 - "Growth"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 4.0: Contact
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Tech
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Clear a Path: Navigate through an escalating series of Cluster Alarms (Class II through Class VI+) to reach the forward extraction point. Initial encounters feature typical enemies, but difficulty gradually transforms into massive hordes of Chargers. Zone 100 contains a large concentration of Big Chargers (extremely dangerous). Zone 101 has a sleeping Tank. Extraction from Zone 102 triggers an Extraction Alarm spawning four Chargers every ~15 seconds.

### Secondary Objective

Activate Generator Cluster: Locate two Power Cells distributed across zones. Navigate Blood Doors with heightened enemy spawns. Insert both cells into the Generator Cluster. Completion triggers conversion of all existing fog into Infectious Fog and its gradual rise throughout the expedition.

### Overload Objective

Retrieve Item (HISEC Cargo): Access Zone 710 via Surge Alarm. Collect a Bulkhead Key, locate and retrieve HISEC Cargo. Picking up the cargo initiates an Error Alarm spawning three Shadows every ~10 seconds. Transport cargo to extraction point.

## Level Description

R4D2 is the second D-tier expedition and notably takes place in the Tech environment, a departure from the Mines/Research environments common in earlier tiers. The level is defined by its escalating Cluster Alarm progression through the Main sector, starting at Class II and climbing through increasingly dangerous alarm classes. The enemy composition shifts from standard enemies to massive Charger hordes as players push deeper.

The Secondary objective has a dramatic environmental impact -- completing it converts all existing fog to Infectious Fog that gradually rises, adding a persistent time pressure element. The Overload sector introduces Shadow enemies and a continuous Error Alarm upon cargo pickup.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Escalating Cluster Alarms:** Progressive difficulty scaling from Class II through Class VI+ alarms across the Main sector.
- **Charger Escalation:** Enemy composition shifts from standard enemies to massive Charger/Big Charger hordes in later zones.
- **Infectious Fog Conversion:** Completing the Secondary objective converts all fog to Infectious Fog that gradually rises throughout the level.
- **Sleeping Tank:** Zone 101 contains a sleeping Tank that must be dealt with.
- **Error Alarm (Overload):** Picking up HISEC Cargo spawns 3 Shadows every ~10 seconds.
- **Extraction Alarm:** Zone 102 entry spawns 4 Chargers every ~15 seconds.
- **Surge Alarm:** Used for Overload sector access (Class III).
- **Blood Doors:** Featured in Secondary objective zones (347/348/349).
- **Spawnblocking:** Standing in specific zones can prevent enemy spawning from certain locations.
- **Prisoner Efficiency:** Recommended order is Overload, then Secondary, then Main, then cargo retrieval. Speed is critical post-Secondary due to fog escalation.
- **Key Zones:**
  - Zone 95: Optional resource zone
  - Zone 97: Optional dark zone (Class III Surge Alarm, Chargers, two Scouts)
  - Zone 99: Optional dark zone (Class IV Mixed Alarm, Chargers, two Scouts)
  - Zone 100: Extremely dangerous Big Charger concentration
  - Zone 710: Overload zone with Shadows and Shadow Scouts
