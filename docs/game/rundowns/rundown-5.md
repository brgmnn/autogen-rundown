# ALT://Rundown 5.0: Rebirth

## Overview

- **Release Date:** June 15, 2023
- **Tiers:** A, B, C, D, E
- **Total Expeditions:** 13
- **Location:** Floodways / KDS Deep

## Narrative Summary

Widely considered the second hardest rundown in the game. Prisoners explore the Floodways sewer system and ultimately access KDS Deep, where they unlock the Influx Protocol for the Warden. The Warden gains access to KDS (Kovac Defense Services) personnel and data. Evidence suggests other active KSO teams conducting parallel operations. R5C3 reaches the deepest depth at 1,109 meters.

## New Features & Content

- **New Weapons:** Double-Tap Rifle, Sawed-Off Shotgun, Heavy SMG
- **Enemy:** Snatchers introduced to ALT Timeline (originally from Rundown 7.0)
- **Lore Change:** Starting with this rundown, lore logs appear in Secondary and Overload sectors, not exclusively Main sectors
- **Notable:** R5D1 is a remake of R1D1, making it "a remake of a remake of a remake of a remake"
