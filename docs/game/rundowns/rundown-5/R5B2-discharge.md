# R5B2 - "Discharge"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 5.0: Rebirth
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Gather Items: Collect a Bulkhead Key from Zone 79, then gather 9 of 13 available GLP-1 Canisters across Zones 79, 80, 81, 82, 83, and 84. Access to Zone 84 requires the Bulkhead Key with a door controller in Zone 79. Zones 81 and 83 require Power Cells from Zone 84 for entry. Return to Zone 79 for extraction.

### Secondary Objective

Establish Uplink: Use the Bulkhead Key at Zone 82 to access Zone 156. A colored key inside Zone 156 grants access to either Zone 157 or 158. Two uplink stations must be established: one in the accessed zone and one in Zone 156 itself. Completing both uplinks triggers an Error Alarm spawning 3 enemy units every ~50 seconds until extraction, and unlocks access to Zone 85.

### Overload Objective

Gather Items: After completing the secondary objective, enter Zone 85 to retrieve a Fog Turbine. Use another Bulkhead Key to open access to Zone 315. Collect 5 of 7 available OSIP Hormones across Zones 315, 316, and 317. Zones 316 and 317 feature Class IV Alarms. Collect a final Bulkhead Key in Zone 315 before returning.

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Scout
- Hybrid (Main sector)
- Spitter (Secondary/Overload sectors)

## Level Description

Discharge is set in a Research environment across three distinct sectors. The main objective involves a large-scale item collection across multiple zones, with Power Cells gating access to certain areas. The secondary objective requires establishing two uplink connections and triggers a persistent Error Alarm. The overload objective takes players into fog-filled zones requiring a Fog Turbine, collecting biological samples (OSIP Hormones). Infectious fog and Spitters add infection pressure in the later sectors.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Blood Doors:** Present in Zones 80 and 82
- **Alarm Doors:** Class IV Alarms (Zones 316, 317)
- **Error Alarm:** Triggered by secondary objective completion; spawns 3 enemies every ~50 seconds
- **Fog Turbine:** Required for Overload sector traversal
- **Infectious Fog:** Present in Overload sector with Spitters
- **Prisoner Efficiency Route:** Secondary, then Overload, then Main
- **Zone Layout:** Main Zones 79-85; Secondary Zones 156-158; Overload Zones 315-317
- Zone 315 has particularly high resources (180% ammo, 180% disinfection)
