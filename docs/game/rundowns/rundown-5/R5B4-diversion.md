# R5B4 - "Diversion"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 5.0: Rebirth
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Floodways
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Survive Warden Protocol: Survive three escalating Warden Protocol timed events across multiple zones over a 30-minute countdown. Phase 1: Proceed to Zone 179 and survive until the Warden Protocol unlocks the door to Zone 182 (unlocks at 10 minutes). Phase 2: Proceed to Zone 182 and survive until the door to Zone 185 unlocks (at 20 minutes). Phase 3: Survive in Zone 186 until the door to Zone 187 unlocks (at 30 minutes). Head to Zone 187 and escape. Optional side zones (180/181, 183/184) provide additional resources and Power Cells.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Hybrid
- Snatcher (spawns at 20:00 and 10:00 countdown marks)

## Level Description

Diversion is a pure survival mission set in the Floodways environment. Players must endure a 30-minute countdown with escalating enemy waves across three phases, each unlocking a new zone to push forward through. The level implies that the players are serving as a tactical distraction for parallel operations by other KSO teams. Optional side zones gated by Power Cells provide additional resources for teams willing to explore during the countdown. The Snatcher enemy appears at specific countdown timestamps, adding an unpredictable threat.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Warden Protocol Timer:** 30-minute survival with three phase transitions at 10, 20, and 30 minutes
- **Escalating Waves:** Enemy spawns intensify throughout the countdown with tougher enemy groups at specific intervals
- **Infectious Fog:** Rises at key timestamps during the countdown
- **Darkness Events:** ~30 seconds of darkness at the 26:50 mark; ~3 minutes of darkness at the 6:40 mark
- **Snatcher Spawns:** Appear at the 20:00 and 10:00 countdown marks
- **Power Cell Gates:** Optional zones (180/181, 183/184) require Power Cells for access but provide extra resources
- **No Secondary/Overload:** Single-sector survival mission only
- **Zone Layout:** Zones 178-187 with optional side branches
