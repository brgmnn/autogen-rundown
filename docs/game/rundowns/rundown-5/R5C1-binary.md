# R5C1 - "Binary"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 5.0: Rebirth
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Floodways
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Establish Uplink: Establish two 7-code uplinks across different zones. First uplink in Zone 112 or 113, second uplink in Zone 117 or 118. Retrieve a Power Cell and insert it into a Generator in Zone 111. Return to the extraction point.

### Secondary Objective

Establish Uplink: Establish a 10-code uplink in Zone 443 at the specified terminal. Zone 443 contains a sleeping Mother enemy and Shadows. Completing the secondary objective triggers an Error Alarm that escalates from spawning 5 Shadows every ~90 seconds to spawning 1 Big Shadow instead.

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Hybrid
- Scout
- Shadow (Secondary sector)
- Big Shadow (Secondary sector)
- Mother (Secondary sector boss)

## Level Description

Binary is set in the Floodways environment and focuses on establishing uplink connections. The main objective requires two separate uplink defenses, while the secondary objective introduces Shadow enemies and a Mother boss in a confined zone. Completing the secondary objective triggers a persistent Error Alarm that spawns Shadows for the remainder of the expedition, escalating to Big Shadows over time. Strategic door placement is critical for controlling enemy approach routes during uplink defenses.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Uplink Defense:** Two 7-code uplinks (Main), one 10-code uplink (Secondary)
- **Error Alarm:** Spawns 5 Shadows every ~90 seconds after secondary completion; escalates to 1 Big Shadow
- **Boss Encounter:** Sleeping Mother in Zone 443
- **Respawn Zones:** Zones 111 and 116 feature respawn areas with numerous sleeping Giants; Scouts do not respawn in these zones
- **Door Funneling:** Strategic door placement controls enemy approach routes during uplink defenses
- **Zone Layout:** Main Zones 110-118; Secondary Zone 443 (subdivided into 443A-F)
- Four-player squad recommended for defense positioning
