# R5C3 - "Starvation"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 5.0: Rebirth
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Establish Uplink: Begin in Zone 371 and progress through Zone 372 to Zone 374. Establish two Corrupted Uplinks in Zone 377 using correct terminals. One of the required terminals must be unlocked with a password from a terminal in Zone 378. Completing both uplinks unlocks the secondary objective and initiates an Error Alarm spawning 3 Giants every ~90 seconds.

### Secondary Objective

Process Item: Retrieve a Datasphere from Zone 415, obtain a colored key from Zone 417 or 418, and insert the Datasphere into an NFrame in Zone 419. Upon insertion, all lights in the expedition turn off and a new Error Alarm spawns a wave of 15 Strikers/Shooters. Retrieve the Datasphere and extract it.

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

Starvation is set in a Research environment and is designed around extreme resource scarcity. Ammunition is sparse throughout the level, requiring players to rely on melee combat for sleeping enemies and conserve ammo for alarm encounters. Zone 377 offers the highest ammo concentration (280%) as a relief point after early deprivation. The main objective involves establishing Corrupted Uplinks with password-locked terminals, while the secondary objective plunges the entire expedition into darkness upon Datasphere insertion. The level demands careful resource management and fast execution during the secondary objective to outpace spawning enemies.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Resource Scarcity:** Sparse ammo throughout early zones; melee-focused strategies required until Zone 377
- **Corrupted Uplinks:** Require password discovery from Zone 378 terminals
- **Error Alarm:** Spawns 3 Giants every ~90 seconds after main uplinks complete
- **Total Darkness:** Secondary objective disables all lights in the expedition upon Datasphere insertion
- **Alarm Doors:** Class V, Class VI Sustain, Class VII Mixed alarms
- **Scan Types:** Team Scans (Zone 375); Mixed Scans including 3 Sustain, 2 Big Red, 2 Cluster (Zone 377)
- **Charger Zones:** Zone 375 contains Chargers and a Scout; Zone 376 contains Chargers and a Charger Scout
- **Zone Layout:** Main Zones 371-378; Secondary Zones 415-419
- Zone 415 contains a large quantity of Hybrids
