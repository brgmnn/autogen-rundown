# R5E1 - "KDS Deep"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 5.0: Rebirth
- **Tier:** E
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Terminal Command (Initiate Influx Protocol): Navigate 18 sequential zones (528-545), collecting keys, inserting Power Cells into generators, and managing multiple alarm scenarios. Issue the command "DEACTIVATE_ALARMS" to disable active alarm systems, and ultimately execute "KDS_DEEP_CRISIS_OVERRIDE" at a terminal in Zone 545. Complete the extraction scan to finish the expedition.

None

## Level Description

KDS Deep is the final expedition of Rundown 5 and the only E-tier level. Players begin with 100% infection that soft-caps at 85% outside fog zones, effectively limiting health to 15% from the start. Infection Resistance boosters cannot reduce starting infection but may help in fog zones with sufficient stacking. The expedition spans 18 zones (528-545) in a gauntlet-style progression through the KDS Deep sector of the Mines. The mission culminates in initiating the Influx Protocol via a special terminal command. Three lore logs are scattered throughout (Zones 528, 534, 540). Scouts found during this mission spawn exclusively Giants, Hybrids, and Big Shooters if alerted.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Starting Infection:** Players begin at 100% infection (soft-caps at 85% outside fog), capping health at ~15%
- **Alarm Deactivation:** Terminal command "DEACTIVATE_ALARMS" introduced; allows disabling active alarms
- **Error Alarms:** Multiple zones with Error Alarms spawning enemies every ~30-50 seconds
- **Ambush Mechanic:** Enemies spawn when approaching certain security doors
- **Infectious Fog:** Zone 529 features dense infectious fog with Spitters, reducing health to 1%
- **Fog Repellers:** Found exclusively in Resource Boxes/Lockers in Zone 529
- **Fog Turbine:** Available in Zone 539 for infection management
- **Blood Door:** Zone 542 spawns a Snatcher
- **Key Zones:**
  - Zone 530: Class III Full Team Alarm with Disinfection Station
  - Zone 532: Class III Surge Alarm with ~12 Giants and 2-use Disinfection Pack
  - Zone 533: Error Alarm spawning 5 enemies every ~30 seconds
  - Zone 535: 2 Mothers and Baby Strikers
  - Zone 536: Multiple Chargers, Big Chargers, Charger Scout, Tank
  - Zone 538: Error Alarm spawning 4 Chargers every ~50 seconds
  - Zone 541: Class V Mixed Alarm spawning exclusively Shadows
  - Zone 543: Class V Mixed Alarm spawning Giants/Hybrids
  - Zone 544: Class IV Surge Alarm with Snatcher ambush
  - Zone 545: Final zone with extraction scan
- **Cell Skip:** Optional advanced strategy bypassing intended deactivation sequences
- **Zone Layout:** Zones 528-545; single sector, 18 sequential zones
- Zone 537 has highest ammo (400%); Zones 541, 543 have highest medical (120-200%)
