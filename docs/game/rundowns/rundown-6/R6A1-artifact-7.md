# R6A1 - "Artifact 7"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Main Sector
- **Dimensions:** Yes - picking up the Matter Wave Projector in Zone 80 briefly teleports the team to the Alpha Environment before returning to Zone 80

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Retrieve Item: Navigate to Zone 76 or 77 and acquire a colored Key to unlock the Security Door to Zone 78. Proceed through Zone 78 (Class II Alarm, Checkpoint) and Zone 79 (Class III Alarm) to Zone 80 where the Matter Wave Projector is collected. Picking it up triggers a brief teleportation to the Alpha Environment. Upon return to Zone 80, Zone 81 unlocks and an Error Alarm begins spawning 12 Strikers/Shooters every ~35 seconds for the remainder of the level. Carry the Matter Wave Projector to Zone 81 and extract.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

Artifact 7 is the opening level of Rundown 6 and serves as the introduction to the Matter Wave Projector, a key item that recurs throughout the rundown. Players start in Zone 75 and progress through branching paths (Zone 76 and 77) to acquire a colored key. The level escalates through Class II and Class III alarm doors before reaching the Projector in Zone 80. Collecting the Projector triggers a brief dimensional teleportation -- the first taste of the dimension mechanic that defines Rundown 6. Upon returning, an Error Alarm creates relentless pressure as the team must extract through Zone 81 while carrying the Projector.

This level follows directly from ALT://R5E1 and leads into ALT://R6AX.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Matter Wave Projector:** First introduction of this key equipment item for Rundown 6. Picking it up triggers dimensional teleportation.
- **Checkpoint Scan:** Formally reintroduced after first appearing in ALT://R1A1.
- **Class II & III Alarms:** Multiple alarm sequences during zone progression.
- **Error Alarm:** Continuous spawning of 12 Strikers/Shooters every ~35 seconds after collecting the Projector, lasting until extraction.
- **Branching Paths:** Zones 76 and 77 offer a choice; one contains the required key, the other provides additional resources.

## Strategy Notes

- Mine and close doors at Zones 76A/77A to delay enemy waves during the Error Alarm.
- C-Foam Launcher recommended for reinforcing doors during the Class III alarm.
- Deplete all resource packs before collecting the Matter Wave Projector.
- Place mines and spray foam strategically before the final defense and extraction.
