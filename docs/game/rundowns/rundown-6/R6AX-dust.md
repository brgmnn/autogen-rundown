# R6AX - "Dust"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** A (Extended Expedition)
- **Environment:** Main Sector
- **Dimensions:** Yes - team is transported to the Alpha Three Installation during the objective sequence

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Terminal Command: Locate the Alpha Three Installation through a series of terminal commands. Navigate to Zone 13 (Class IV Alarm), progress through Zone 14 (populated exclusively with Giants and Big Shooters), reach Zone 15 (Class IV Alarm) where a locked terminal requires a password found in Zone 20. Retrieve a colored Key from Zone 21 (behind a Blood Door). Access Zone 20 (Checkpoint) and find the password `OVERRIDE_SECURITY_LOCKDOWN`. Input the command at Zone 15's terminal, triggering a Room Scan with enemy waves. Navigate to Zone 18 via Zone 17 and input `EXTRACT_ALPHA_THREE_COORDINATES`. Enter the Alpha Three Installation and input `FORCE_SHUTDOWN`, triggering a final Room Scan. Extract via Zone 19.

None

None

## Level Description

Dust is the extended A-tier expedition for Rundown 6. Despite its A-tier placement, it is rated closer to B-tier difficulty. The level involves a complex series of terminal commands across many zones, culminating in a dimensional shift to the Alpha Three Installation. Zone 14 is notable for containing exclusively Giants and Big Shooters. Zone 18 has 4 patrolling Scouts that must be carefully navigated. The final Room Scan in the Alpha Three Installation introduces Flyers and Big Flyers -- the Big Flyers should be prioritized as kill targets.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Multiple Class IV Alarms:** Zones 13 and 15 both feature Class IV Alarm doors.
- **Blood Door:** Zone 21 requires clearing a Blood Door.
- **Checkpoint:** Zone 20 serves as a checkpoint.
- **Terminal Command Sequences:** Multiple terminal commands required for progression (`OVERRIDE_SECURITY_LOCKDOWN`, `EXTRACT_ALPHA_THREE_COORDINATES`, `FORCE_SHUTDOWN`).
- **Room Scans:** Enemy waves spawn during Room Scan sequences at terminals.
- **Dimensional Shift:** Team is transported to Alpha Three Installation during the final objective phase.
- **Flyers/Big Flyers:** Unique enemy types encountered during the Alpha Three phase.
- **Scout Patrols:** Zone 18 features 4 patrolling Scouts requiring stealth or careful engagement.

## Strategy Notes

- C-Foam Launcher offers minimal utility in this level.
- Recommend 1-2 precision weapons (non-shotgun/automatic) for dealing with Big Flyers.
- Sentry placement is critical for alarm management.
- Building on the south side of Alpha Three offers an optimal defensive position for the final Room Scan.
- Priority target: eliminate Big Flyers first during the final Room Scan.
