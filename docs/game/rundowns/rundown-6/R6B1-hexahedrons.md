# R6B1 - "Hexahedrons"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Main Sector
- **Dimensions:** Yes - Matter Wave Platform in Zone 38 teleports the team to the Alpha Two Installation for a terminal command and Flyer defense sequence

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Terminal Command: Transport the Matter Wave Projector to Zone 38 and execute a terminal command in an alternate dimension. Progress from Zone 31 through Zone 32 (Class IV Diminished Alarm) to retrieve a Power Cell, insert it into the Generator in Zone 31B to unlock Zone 33. Navigate through Zone 33 to Zone 34 (Checkpoint), then collect a colored Key from Zone 35 or 36 (Class IV Alarm) to open Zone 37 (Class V Diminished Alarm). Enter Zone 38 (Checkpoint), insert the Matter Wave Projector into the pedestal, and complete a Team Scan. This teleports the team to the Alpha Two Installation where they must locate a terminal and input `INTERPRET_NAV_DATA`, then defend against Flyer waves for approximately 35 seconds. Upon return to Zone 38, an Incursion begins spawning 10 Flyers every ~30 seconds. Retreat to Zone 31 and extract.

## Level Description

Hexahedrons introduces the Matter Wave Platform mechanic, building on the Matter Wave Projector from R6A1. The level requires carrying the Projector through increasingly difficult alarm sequences before inserting it into a pedestal in Zone 38. The Team Scan teleports the squad to the Alpha Two Installation -- a dimensional space where they encounter Flyers for the first time. After inputting the terminal command and surviving ~35 seconds of Flyer waves, the team returns to Zone 38 but must then fight through continuous Flyer incursions (10 every ~30 seconds) while retreating all the way back to Zone 31 for extraction.

The Flyer's weak point is its glowing red mouth.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Matter Wave Projector & Platform:** The Projector is carried to Zone 38 and inserted into a platform pedestal to enable dimensional travel.
- **Diminished Alarms:** Modified alarm events in Zones 32 (Class IV) and 37 (Class V) with reduced difficulty.
- **Alpha Two Installation:** Alternate dimension accessed via the Matter Wave Platform.
- **Flyer Introduction:** First encounter with Flyers in this rundown's progression; they appear in the Alpha Two Installation.
- **Post-Dimensional Incursion:** After returning from Alpha Two, continuous Flyer spawning (10 every ~30 seconds) until extraction.
- **Power Cell Mechanic:** Retrieve Power Cell from Zone 32 and insert into Generator in Zone 31B.
- **Checkpoints:** Zones 34 and 38.

## Strategy Notes

- All tools have utility throughout the expedition.
- The Matter Wave Projector doesn't need to be constantly carried -- it can be set down.
- Door management and defensive positions are critical during alarm sequences.
- Build cover for Flyer engagements; target the glowing red mouth.
- Resource management is important for the sustained defense during retreat.
