# R6B2 - "Contaminant"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Main Sector + Secondary Sector
- **Dimensions:** No - standard zone progression without alternate dimension mechanics

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Retrieve HSU: Navigate through zones 59-67, acquiring a Bulkhead Key from Zone 65, and scanning an HSU device in Zone 64. Progression involves Zone 59 (Class IV Alarm), optional Zone 58 for resources, Zone 60 (Class III Alarm) for a colored Key, Zone 61 (Blood Door), Zone 62 (Checkpoint), Zone 63, Zone 65 (Class IV Alarm) for the Bulkhead Key, Zone 64 (Class IV Alarm) for the HSU scan. Completing the scan triggers an Error Alarm spawning 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~30 seconds. Extract through Zones 66-67 (Blood Door).

### Secondary Objective

Establish Uplink: Located in the secondary sector. Complete 4-Code Uplinks in Zones 224/225 after entering Zone 223 (Checkpoint). Both Zones 224 and 225 have Blood Doors.

None

## Level Description

Contaminant is a B-tier level featuring both Main and Secondary sectors connected by a Bulkhead Door. The Main objective requires navigating through fog-filled zones to locate and scan an HSU device, which triggers a persistent Error Alarm for the extraction phase. The Secondary objective in Zones 223-225 involves completing 4-Code Uplinks behind Blood Doors. Infectious Fog is a major environmental hazard throughout, requiring Fog Repellers for safe traversal. Zone 58 is optional but provides valuable extra resources including a Scout that must be dealt with carefully.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Infectious Fog:** Present in multiple zones; Fog Repellers recommended for traversal.
- **Blood Doors:** Zones 61, 67 (main), and Zones 224-225 (secondary) feature Blood Doors.
- **Class III & IV Alarms:** Multiple alarm doors throughout the main sector.
- **Bulkhead Doors:** Separates main and secondary sectors; Zone 64/223 split.
- **Error Alarm:** Triggered after HSU scan completion, spawning 4 Strikers/Shooters every ~30 seconds.
- **Checkpoint:** Zone 62 (main), Zone 223 (secondary).
- **Disinfection:** Disinfectant resources available in some zones due to fog infection.

## Resources

Zone 58 (optional) provides high resources: 80% Ammo, 80% Medi, 60% Tools. Other zones have varying distributions. Disinfectant is available in fog-heavy zones.

## Strategy Notes

- Mine Deployer is useful for alarm management.
- Conserve Fog Repellers through careful navigation.
- Place sentries strategically at doorways during alarms.
- C-Foam reinforcement recommended for the extraction phase during the Error Alarm.
- Secondary objective can be used to skip main alarms if coordinated properly.
