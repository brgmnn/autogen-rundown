# R6BX - "Flux"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** B (Extended Expedition)
- **Environment:** Main Sector + Secondary Sector
- **Dimensions:** No - standard zone progression, though features Shadows and Big Shadows as key enemy types

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Terminal Command: Navigate through 12 main zones (180-191) executing power-related terminal commands. Key steps:

1. Zone 181 (Class V Cluster Alarm)
2. Zone 182 triggers a 60-second blackout spawning ~8 Shadows
3. Zone 184 (Class IV Cluster Alarm) - execute `REROUTE_POWER_TO_GATE_CONTROLLER`, initiating an 80-second timer; failure spawns 2 Big Shadows every ~1 minute
4. Locate four terminals in Zone 185 containing password information
5. Return to Zone 183 and input `EMERGENCY_POWER_SUPPLY_ON` to halt the alarm
6. Zone 186 triggers an Error Alarm spawning 3 Shadows every ~30 seconds
7. Find 4 password logs from terminals in Zones 186-189 (Blood Doors spawn Shadows upon entry)
8. Return to Zone 183 southeastern bunker terminal and input `EMERGENCY_POWER_SUPPLY_ON`
9. Zone 190 (Class I Sustain Alarm, Checkpoint) - locate Bulkhead Key
10. Unlock Zone 191 and extract

### Secondary Objective

Survive Warden Protocol: Time-based survival challenge in secondary zones 249-252. Unlock Zone 249 using Bulkhead Key, reach Zone 250 (Class III Cluster Alarm), retrieve a Power Cell, deliver it to Generator in Zone 249, enter Zone 251 and survive the Warden Protocol.

**Warden Protocol Timeline:**

- 10:00 - Spawns ~15 Strikers/Shooters every 90 seconds
- 08:00 - Giant group spawn
- 07:10 - Lights disable; ~12 Shadow spawn (Strikers/Shooters cease)
- 05:30 - ~12 Shadow spawn
- 03:50 - Big Shadow group
- 02:30 - ~12 Shadow spawn
- 01:45 - ~12 Shadow spawn
- 0:00 - Zone 252 unlocks; Error Alarm spawns ~15 Shadows per minute through expedition end

## Level Description

Flux is the extended B-tier expedition, notable for its heavy use of Shadows and darkness mechanics. The main sector involves a complex series of power-restoration terminal commands across 12 zones with frequent backtracking. Zone 182's entry triggers a dramatic 60-second blackout with Shadow ambushes. Blood Doors in Zones 187-189 each spawn Shadows when opened. The secondary sector features a brutal Warden Protocol survival event with escalating enemy waves transitioning from standard enemies to Shadows and Big Shadows as lights are disabled. After the protocol completes, an Error Alarm spawns ~15 Shadows per minute for the remainder of the expedition.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Darkness/Blackout:** Zone 182 entry causes a 60-second light shutdown with Shadow ambush.
- **Blood Doors:** Zones 187-189 spawn Shadows when opened.
- **Error Alarms:** Multiple power-related alarms trigger spawning patterns throughout.
- **Terminal Commands:** `REROUTE_POWER_TO_GATE_CONTROLLER` and `EMERGENCY_POWER_SUPPLY_ON` required for progression and alarm management.
- **Cluster Alarms:** Class V, Class IV, and Class III variants.
- **Warden Protocol:** Timed survival event in secondary sector with detailed wave timeline.
- **Shadows & Big Shadows:** Dominant enemy type requiring Bio Tracker awareness.
- **Checkpoint:** Zone 190.

## Strategy Notes

- Two Sentries recommended over C-Foam Launcher for main objective.
- Bio Tracker is essential for Shadow-heavy encounters.
- Door foaming/mining is effective for alarm management.
- Password guessing is possible after 2-3 letters are identified from terminals.
- Large Zone 250 benefits from pinging to locate the Power Cell.
- Secondary objective allows pre-loading the main bulkhead scan.
