# R6C3 - "Pressure Point"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Main Sector + Secondary Sector + Overload Sector
- **Dimensions:** No - standard zone progression without dimensional mechanics

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Survive Warden Protocol: Endure a 30-minute Warden Protocol event. Progress from Zone 170 (Blood Door) through Zones 171-178, collecting a Bulkhead Key from Zone 171. The Security Door to Zone 179 unlocks after 30 minutes have elapsed. Extract via Zone 179.

**Warden Protocol Events Timeline (17 events over 30 minutes):**

- 26:50 - Darkness Event: all expedition lights deactivate for 2 minutes
- 21:40 - Fog Initiation: Infectious Fog begins appearing
- 20:00, 16:00, 8:00 - Fog increase intervals
- 15:00 - Hybrids become available as spawned enemies
- 5:50 - Extended Darkness: lights off for 3 minutes

### Secondary Objective

Activate Generator Cluster: Collect three Power Cells distributed across Zones 250, 176, and 177. Return all cells to the Generator Cluster in Zone 250 for insertion. This unlocks additional passages and provides resource-gathering opportunities.

### Overload Objective

Terminal Command: Access Zone 305 via a Bulkhead Key from Zone 171. Input `SCP_ZONE_PURGE` at a terminal. Complete a special Large Team Scan requiring 120 seconds (does not scale with player count). Collect a Bulkhead Key from Zone 306 while a sleeping Tank is present. The scan triggers subsequent spawning events at 4-8 minute intervals.

## Level Description

Pressure Point is a survival-focused C-tier level built around a 30-minute Warden Protocol countdown. Rather than rushing through zones, players must methodically clear and hold territory while managing escalating environmental hazards. The protocol features 17 distinct events including darkness periods, rising infectious fog, and evolving enemy compositions. Zone 172 contains sleeping enemies that can be eliminated via stealth melee. The level is designed so that main objective zones require approximately 5 minutes each, but completing secondary and overload objectives reduces available time per zone. Achieving Prisoner Efficiency (all three objectives) requires completing the Overload objective before the Main objective's final stages.

## Notable Mechanics

- **30-Minute Warden Protocol:** Timed survival with 17 scripted events including darkness, fog, and enemy escalation.
- **Darkness Events:** Lights deactivate at 26:50 (2 minutes) and 5:50 (3 minutes).
- **Infectious Fog:** Steadily increases at multiple intervals (21:40, 20:00, 16:00, 8:00).
- **Blood Door:** Zone 170 entry.
- **Class I Alarm:** Zone 175 checkpoint.
- **Sleeping Enemies:** Zone 172 allows stealth melee elimination.
- **Large Team Scan:** Overload objective's 120-second scan does not scale with player count.
- **Tank:** Sleeping in Zone 306 (overload sector).
- **Closeable Doors:** Multiple throughout zones enable Mine Deployer defensive strategies.

## Strategy Notes

- Mine Deployer is the most useful tool for defensive door placement.
- C-Foam Launcher delays enemy waves; Sentries supplement when doors are not available.
- Main objective requires approximately 5 minutes per zone maximum.
- Each additional objective reduces average time per zone by one minute.
- For Tank encounters, surround the creature to enable consistent tumor targeting.
- Zone 178C stairs provide advantageous positioning against spawned Tanks during extraction.
- Prisoner Efficiency requires completing Overload before Main objective's final stages.
