# R6CX - "Ascent"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** C (Extended Expedition)
- **Environment:** Research Sector (Main)
- **Dimensions:** Yes - completing the first Uplink in Zone 81 teleports the team to the Alpha Three Installation for 3 additional Uplinks, then teleports back to Zone 79

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Establish Uplink: A gauntlet-style expedition requiring teams to overcome sequential challenges until the final Uplink objective is reached.

1. Zone 77 (Class V Mixed Alarm) - contains 4 Scouts, 1 Charger Scout, multiple Giants
2. Zone 78 (Class III Stealth Scan, Checkpoint)
3. Zone 79 (Class V Alarm) - power failure causes total darkness; high concentration of Hybrids
4. Zone 80 - power restoration; spawns 2 Charger Scouts and 5 Scouts
5. Zone 81 (Checkpoint) - establish first 4-Code Uplink at terminal, which teleports team to Alpha Three Installation
6. Complete 3 additional 4-Code Uplinks at terminals within Alpha Three Installation
7. Zone 82 - confirm Unit 27 status by viewing corpses in upper southwestern corner; triggers Zone 83 door opening
8. Zone 83 - extract

## Level Description

Ascent is the extended C-tier expedition, functioning as a linear gauntlet where each zone presents a distinct challenge that must be overcome before progressing. The level features a dramatic power failure in Zone 79 plunging the team into total darkness with Hybrids, followed by power restoration in Zone 80 that reveals Charger Scouts and regular Scouts. The dimensional shift occurs at Zone 81 where completing the first Uplink teleports the team to the Alpha Three Installation for three more Uplinks. An Incursion event spawns Flyers and Big Flyers during the Alpha Three phase. After returning, the team must confirm Unit 27's status by locating corpses in Zone 82 before extraction.

The level features fog infection as an additional environmental hazard.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Class V Mixed Alarm:** Zone 77 with Scouts, Charger Scouts, and Giants.
- **Class III Stealth Scan:** Zone 78.
- **Power Failure/Darkness:** Zone 79 triggers complete darkness.
- **Power Restoration:** Zone 80 restores lighting but spawns Charger Scouts and Scouts.
- **Dimensional Shift:** First Uplink completion teleports to Alpha Three Installation; team returns to Zone 79 afterward.
- **4-Code Uplinks:** Four total (1 in Zone 81, 3 in Alpha Three).
- **Incursion Event:** Flyer/Big Flyer waves during Alpha Three Uplinks; Strikers, Shooters, and Hybrids also spawn during individual Uplink completion.
- **Fog Infection:** Present throughout the expedition.
- **Checkpoints:** Zones 78 and 81.
- **Unit 27 Confirmation:** Zone 82 requires viewing corpses to trigger Zone 83 door.
