# R6D2 - "Crossways"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Main Sector + Secondary Sector
- **Dimensions:** No - standard zone progression, though secondary sector features massive Flyer waves

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Terminal Command: Locate 9 of 12 available terminals distributed across multiple zones and execute data extraction. Key terminal locations include:

- Terminal 1: Zone 192 (free at start)
- Terminals 2-3: Zone 195 (surrounded by 6 Charger Scouts)
- Terminal 4: Zone 205 (Mother presence + Giant blood door)
- Terminal 5: Zone 206 (Mother presence + Hybrid blood door)
- Terminal 6: Zone 200b (deactivates Error Alarm)
- Terminals 7-8: Zones 198c, 199c (Error Alarm active)
- Terminal 9: Zone 197b (1 Charger Scout)
- Terminals 10-12: Zones 202b, 202b, 203a (optional; heavily defended)

### Secondary Objective

Establish Uplink: Corrupted Uplink (Dual Terminal Uplink) in secondary zones 363-366. Enter Zone 363 via Bulkhead Door. Optional Zone 364 allows executing a Ventilation Sequence to clear infectious fog. Zone 366 spawns massive Flyer waves; acquire password from terminal. Activate 4-Code Uplink on two Zone 363 terminals. A Tank spawns in Zone 365 upon completion.

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

Crossways is a D-tier level featuring a sprawling main sector with 15 zones (192-206) where players must locate and interact with 9 of 12 terminals for data extraction. The level is notable for its Charger-heavy enemy composition, with Charger Scouts being a significant threat in Zone 195 and elsewhere. Two Mother enemies guard terminals in Zones 205 and 206 behind Blood Doors. An Error Alarm in Zones 198-199 spawns 3 Chargers every 40 seconds until deactivated at the Zone 200b terminal. The secondary sector features a Corrupted Uplink requiring dual terminal operation, massive Flyer waves in Zone 366, and a Tank spawn upon completion.

Zones 201-203 are heavily defended and generally advised to avoid.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Class III Alarm:** Zone 195.
- **Class V Cluster Alarm:** Zone 199.
- **Error Alarm:** Zones 198-199 spawn 3 Chargers every 40 seconds; deactivated at Zone 200b terminal.
- **Checkpoint Scan:** Zone 197.
- **Stealth Scans:** Zones 201-203.
- **Class IV Mixed Alarm:** Zone 366 (secondary).
- **Blood Doors:** Multiple zones requiring specific creature kills (Giant, Hybrid, Charger).
- **Infectious Fog:** Present in zones; clearable via Ventilation Sequence in Zone 364.
- **Fog Turbine:** Optional in Zone 193.
- **Bulkhead Doors:** Key progression points between sectors.
- **Corrupted Uplink:** Dual terminal mechanic requiring coordinated team operation.
- **Silent Extraction:** Final objective extraction method.
- **Mother Encounters:** Zones 205 and 206.

## Resources

Zone 195 contains a large resource concentration: 80 ammo, 180 medicine, 120 tools, 160 disinfectant. Other zones have varying distributions.

## Strategy Notes

- Avoid Zones 201-203 due to extreme difficulty.
- C-Foam Launcher is useful for stealth-eliminating Charger Scouts.
- Sentries recommended at doorways during major alarms.
- Use cover (pillars, closed doors) and mines strategically.
- Bot teams may struggle significantly with the dual terminal uplink mechanic.
- Bulkhead Key must be located in Zone 197 to access the final door.
