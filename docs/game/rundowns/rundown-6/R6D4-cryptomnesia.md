# R6D4 - "Cryptomnesia"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** ALT://Rundown 6.0: Destination
- **Tier:** D (final expedition of Rundown 6)
- **Environment:** Four distinct dimensions with similar layouts but different challenges
- **Dimensions:** Yes - this is the most dimension-heavy level in Rundown 6. The expedition spans five dimensional variations, each with unique enemy compositions and environmental hazards. Collecting Tampered Data Cubes triggers teleportation between dimensions.

## Objectives

### Main Objective

Gather Items: Collect Tampered Data Cubes across four different dimensions, each with unique enemy compositions and hazards. The expedition cycles through five dimensional variations:

#### Dimension 1: Error

- Error Alarm spawning 4 Strikers/Shooters/Flyers every 30 seconds
- Zone 410 -> Zone 412 (Blood Door) -> Zone 416 (Class IV Surge Alarm)
- Colored Key in Zone 410; Power Cell from Zone 414
- Tampered Data Cube in Zone 416
- Enemies: Strikers, Shooters, Flyers, Mother

#### Dimension 2: Giant

- No alarm doors
- Zone 410 -> Zone 412 -> Zone 413 (Checkpoint)
- Tampered Data Cube in Zone 413
- Enemies: Giants (multiple), Shooters, Big Shooters, 6 Scouts, Baby Strikers, Mother

#### Dimension 3: Charger

- T Scan in Zone 414
- Zone 410 -> Zone 412 (Blood Door) -> Zone 414
- Colored Key in Zone 413
- Tampered Data Cube in Zone 414
- Enemies: Chargers (multiple), Tank, Big Chargers, 3 Charger Scouts

#### Dimension 4: Spitter

- Completely submerged in Infectious Fog
- Class V Alarm in Zone 411
- Zone 410 -> Zone 411 (with detour through Zone 412)
- Colored Key in Zone 412; 2 Fog Turbines in Zone 410; Disinfection Station in Zone 412
- Tampered Data Cube in Zone 411
- Enemies: Spitters, Strikers, Shooters, Giants

#### Dimension 5: Surge

- No enemies or Infectious Fog initially
- Doors to Zones 415 and 419 unlocked; Zone 419 has Disinfection Station and extra resources
- Zone 415 (Error Surge Alarm) -> Zone 417 (requires Power Cell) -> Zone 418 (extraction)
- Constant spawns of Strikers, Shooters, occasional Hybrids and Flyers

## Level Description

Cryptomnesia is the final expedition of Rundown 6, featuring a unique layout that spans four (plus one) distinct dimensions sharing similar zone layouts but with radically different challenges. Each dimension is themed around a specific enemy type or hazard: Error (alarm-based with Flyers), Giant (stealth-heavy with massive enemies), Charger (fast aggressive enemies with a Tank), and Spitter (fog-drenched with infection hazards). Collecting Tampered Data Cubes in each dimension teleports the team to the next. The fifth dimension (Surge) serves as the extraction path with a constant-spawn Error Surge Alarm.

The name "Cryptomnesia" refers to a psychological phenomenon where an old memory is mistaken for a new or original thought -- such as a musician who unintentionally copies a song they have previously heard. This is reflected in the level's design: familiar zone layouts that feel like deja vu but are fundamentally altered by each dimension's unique threats.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Multi-Dimensional Travel:** Five dimensional variations traversed sequentially via Tampered Data Cube collection.
- **Shared Zone Layout:** Each dimension uses similar zone numbers (410-419) but with different enemy compositions, hazards, and door configurations.
- **Blood Doors:** Present in Error and Charger dimensions.
- **Infectious Fog:** Environmental hazard unique to the Spitter dimension (completely submerged).
- **Fog Turbines:** Two available in Spitter dimension Zone 410.
- **Disinfection Stations:** Available in Spitter dimension (Zone 412) and Surge dimension (Zone 419).
- **Error Surge Alarm:** Dimension 5 features constant enemy spawns during extraction.
- **Checkpoint:** Located in Giant dimension (Zone 413).
- **Power Cells & Colored Keys:** Required for door progression across multiple dimensions.
- **T Scan:** Charger dimension Zone 414.
- **Class IV Surge Alarm:** Error dimension Zone 416.
- **Class V Alarm:** Spitter dimension Zone 411.
