# Rundown 7.0: Rise

## Overview

- **Release Date:** June 16, 2022
- **Tiers:** A, B, C, D, E
- **Total Expeditions:** 10
- **Location:** Gardens

## Narrative Summary

This rundown focuses on processing a Neonate HSU to convert it into a Conduit. Schaeffer directs prisoners to input commands on a terminal to bring two HSUs to him for rebellion against The Warden. When opening one containing engineer Maddox, a malfunction causes him to attack Schaeffer, who kills him. Players then depressurize the Neonate, locate preparation sites via uplinks, and awaken engineer Klas Henriksson. The narrative involves injecting a Trojan into the Biocom mainframe system to regain control from The Warden, culminating in an ambiguous ending where the Biocom system is hacked by the duo.

## New Features & Content

- **New Weapons:** Precision Rifle, PDW, High Caliber Pistol, Thermal Scope, HEL Shotgun
- **New Enemies:** Snatcher, Immortal (final boss)
- **New Environment:** Gardens
- **New Mechanics:** Timed Sequence, Training/Tutorial expedition, Biotracker Symbiosis, updated music system
- **New Objective Items:** Collection Case, GLP-2 Canister, Memory Stick
- **First rundown** exclusively following the OG storyline rather than the ALT:// team narrative
