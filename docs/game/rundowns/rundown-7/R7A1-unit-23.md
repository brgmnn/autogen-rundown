# R7A1 - "Unit 23"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Retrieve and process a Neonate HSU through a Neurogenic Cardiac Resuscitationer (NCR). Players navigate from Zone 43 through Zone 49, first entering Zone 44 to input the terminal command `RECYCLE_GROUP_7A42`, then collecting a colored key from Zone 46 or 47 (Class II Alarm) to access Zone 48 (checkpoint). Proceed to Zone 49 (Class III Diminished Alarm) and insert the Neonate HSU into the NCR. Processing the Neonate triggers an error alarm spawning 3 Strikers/Shooters or combinations with Giants every 20 seconds. Extract with the processed item back to the entry point.

### Secondary Objective

None

None

## Level Description

R7A1 is the first expedition of Rundown 7 and serves as the introduction to the rundown's storyline. Players are tasked with processing a biological specimen (Neonate HSU) through the Complex's medical equipment. The level involves terminal hacking in Zone 44 and navigating through security doors and checkpoints across Zones 43-49.

This expedition marks the beginning of Schaeffer's communication with the prisoners, outlining efforts to awaken other imprisoned individuals within the Complex. In Zone 45, Schaeffer awakens prisoner Maddox as part of the narrative sequence.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Terminal Hacking:** Zone 44 requires inputting the command `RECYCLE_GROUP_7A42`
- **Class II Alarm:** Triggered when accessing Zone 46/47 for the colored key
- **Class III Diminished Alarm:** Guards the entrance to Zone 49
- **Error Alarm:** Processing the Neonate initiates a continuous error alarm spawning enemies every 20 seconds
- **Checkpoints:** Zone 48 serves as a checkpoint
