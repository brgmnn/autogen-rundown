# R7B1 - "Cargo"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Gardens
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Collect the Neonate HSU at the entry point and process it through a Hydrostasis Depressurizer. Players obtain a colored key from Zone 89/90 (Class III Diminished Alarm) to unlock Zone 91, progress through Zone 92 (checkpoint), then navigate to Zone 94 for a Power Cell to activate a generator. Retrieve a Collection Case from Zone 93 (Class IV Diminished Alarm) and insert it into an Orange Shuttle Box. Insert the Neonate HSU into the Hydrostasis Depressurizer in Zone 96 (Class III Alarm). Extract via Zone 97 (Class T Alarm scan -- only one player needs to be in the scan).

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Scout
- Giant
- Big Shooter
- Flyer (spawns during Zone 96 and Zone 97 alarms)

## Level Description

R7B1 takes place in the Gardens environment, described as containing abundant plant life with overhead walkways. Schaeffer contacts the team requesting delivery of a Collection Case from the area. The level introduces players to the Gardens tileset and involves a multi-step process of transporting and processing the Neonate HSU through the facility's equipment.

The level spans Zones 88-97 with varying resource distributions across each zone. Scout patrols are present in Zones 89/90 and 91, and Big Shooters can spawn hibernating in Zone 92.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Class III Diminished Alarm:** Guards access to Zones 89/90 for key retrieval
- **Class IV Diminished Alarm:** Protects Zone 93 containing the Collection Case
- **Class III Alarm:** Triggered at Zone 96 during HSU processing
- **Class T Traveling Scan:** Zone 97 extraction alarm -- only one player needs to complete the scan
- **Checkpoints:** Zones 92 and 95 serve as checkpoint locations
- **Flyer Spawns:** Flyers emerge when inserting the HSU into the depressurizer and during extraction
- **Scout Patrols:** Present in Zones 89/90 and 91, requiring stealth or careful engagement
- **Strategic Door Management:** Doors can be used for funneling enemies during alarm sequences
