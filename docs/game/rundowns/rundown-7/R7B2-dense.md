# R7B2 - "Dense"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Establish Uplink:** Navigate through Zones 123-131, establishing two uplinks while dealing with various alarm types and security doors. The expedition culminates in an extraction from Zone 131, which has a high concentration of sleeping enemies. Zone 130 requires inputting the terminal command `SECURITY_BYPASS`.

### Secondary Objective

**Gather Items:** Branching from Zone 126, players access Zones 253-255 to collect 4 GLP-1 Canisters. This section features exclusive Shadow encounters. Triggering the Class III alarm in this area permanently disables all expedition lighting for the remainder of the level.

### Overload Objective

None

## Level Description

R7B2 is defined by its extremely thick fog -- far thicker than in most other expeditions -- requiring extensive use of Fog Repellers for visibility. The level takes place in the Mines environment and challenges players with navigating through dense fog conditions while establishing uplink connections.

The main sector spans Zones 122-131, while the secondary sector adds Zones 253-255. Zone 129 offers the highest resource concentration (100% ammo, 140% medi, 80% tool), while the extraction Zone 131 contains zero resources. The secondary objective's lights-out mechanic combined with Shadow enemies creates a particularly intense late-game experience.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Thick Fog:** The defining feature of this level -- far thicker than standard fog, requiring Fog Repellers
- **Blood Doors:** Multiple locked doors requiring mines to breach
- **Security Terminal:** Zone 130 requires inputting `SECURITY_BYPASS` command
- **Class III, IV, and V Alarms:** Various alarm types throughout the main sector
- **Class IV Cluster Alarm:** Present in Zone 124
- **Lights-Out Mechanic:** Completing the secondary objective's Class III alarm permanently disables all lights
- **Shadow Enemies:** Exclusive to the secondary sector, combined with the lights-out mechanic
