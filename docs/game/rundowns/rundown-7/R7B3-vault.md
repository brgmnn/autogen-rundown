# R7B3 - "Vault"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Floodways
- **Dimensions:** Yes (secondary objective uses dimensional transportation for survival sequences)

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Retrieve HSU:** Navigate through Zones 106-112 to locate and scan an HSU. Retrieve colored keys from Zones 114/115 (behind a Blood Door) and Zone 110 to progress. Zone 105 and 108 are Cocoon Respawn Zones. Restore power in Zone 108 and complete a Class IV Cluster Alarm. Upon HSU scan completion, a large wave of enemies spawns followed by continuous error alarms spawning Strikers every ~50 seconds. Extract with the HSU.

### Secondary Objective

**Gather Items:** Access Zone 230 via a bulkhead door and collect three Data Cubes across Zones 230-232. Each Data Cube collection triggers a dimensional survival sequence lasting 60, 120, and 180 seconds respectively, with waves of Strikers, Shooters, and Flyers. Big Flyers appear in the final sequence.

### Overload Objective

**Retrieve Item:** Enter Zone 314 (filled with infectious fog and Chargers) via a bulkhead door in Zone 112. Retrieve a Cargo Crate and extract. Grabbing the cargo initiates additional error alarms spawning 4 Chargers every ~45 seconds. A Fog Turbine can be collected to reduce the infection hazard.

## Level Description

R7B3 takes place in the Floodways environment and features a complex multi-objective structure. The main objective centers on retrieving an HSU through zones guarded by Blood Doors and alarm sequences, while the secondary objective introduces dimensional transportation mechanics where players are teleported to survival arenas after collecting Data Cubes. The overload objective adds infectious fog and Charger enemies for an additional challenge.

The level features Cocoon zones (105, 108) with respawn functionality, and the Floodways setting introduces environmental hazards including infectious fog in the overload section. Resource distribution varies across the main zones, with the secondary zones gradually escalating resources.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Cocoon Respawn Zones:** Zones 105 and 108 feature cocoon mechanics with respawn functionality
- **Blood Doors:** Security doors requiring colored keys, with enemy spawns on breach
- **Class IV Alarms:** Both cluster and standard variants appear
- **Power Outage System:** Zone lighting can be affected by power state
- **Dimensional Transportation:** Secondary objective teleports players to survival arenas for timed sequences (60s, 120s, 180s)
- **Infectious Fog:** Overload zone is filled with infectious fog requiring disinfectant
- **Fog Turbine:** Collectible item that reduces infection hazard in fog areas
- **Bulkhead Doors:** Require controllers and keys to access secondary and overload sectors
- **Error Alarms:** Continuous spawning mechanics during HSU extraction and cargo retrieval
- **C-Foam Limitations:** C-Foam Launcher usage is limited; grenades or sentries recommended
