# R7C1 - "Monster"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Gather Items:** A multi-phase objective requiring players to collect resources across multiple zones.

1. **Initial Phase (Zones 12-22):** Starting in Zone 12, traverse through Zone 20 (with optional Zone 23 containing Scouts and Giant/Big Shooter concentrations), then Zone 21 before triggering an alarm in Zone 22. Retrieve 4 Power Cells and transport them to Zone 12.

2. **Power Cell Distribution (Zones 12-13):** Insert one Power Cell into a Generator in Zone 12 to unlock Zone 13 (checkpoint). In Zone 13, activate at least one of two generators to unlock access to Zone 14 or 17 (or both).

3. **Canister Collection (Zones 14-19):** Collect 9 GLP-2 Canisters distributed across three zones (3 per zone, 18 total available). This phase triggers an Error Alarm spawning enemies every 20-30 seconds.

4. **Final Sequence (Zones 24-28):** Execute command `EMERGENCY_ESCAPE_PROTOCOL` at a terminal in Zone 24 (checkpoint), initiating a Room Scan alarm spawning approximately 12 Strikers/Shooters/Giants per wave, plus 15 additional enemies via active Error Alarm, and seven Snatchers in waves of 1-2 each. Extract through Zone 27 to Zone 28.

## Level Description

R7C1 is notable for being the level that officially introduces the Snatcher enemy type. Snatchers capture team members and relocate them to dangerous areas near other threats, creating team separation that can be devastating during intense combat sequences.

The level features a complex multi-phase structure requiring power cell management and canister collection across a sprawling Research environment. The final sequence is particularly punishing, with stacking enemy waves from both the Room Scan and Error Alarm, combined with Snatcher spawns that threaten to isolate team members.

Zone 24 provides the highest concentrated resources (120 Ammo, 160 Medi, 100 Tool) to prepare for the final sequence, while Zones 25-26 contain no resources.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Snatcher Introduction:** First official appearance of Snatchers, which capture and relocate players
- **Filtration Unit:** Zone 23 contains an optional command `START_FILTRATION_UNIT` to lower fog
- **Class S Large Alarm:** Zone 22 triggers this alarm type spawning Giants and Hybrids
- **Error Alarms:** Multiple phases with escalating spawn rates (30s -> 20s -> 30s per wave)
- **Room Scan:** Final alarm type with stacking enemy waves and Snatcher spawns
- **Checkpoints:** Zones 13 and 24 provide checkpoint saves
- **Power Cell Management:** Strategic placement of 4 Power Cells determines zone access
- **Generator Choice:** Players can choose to unlock Zone 14, Zone 17, or both depending on generator activation
