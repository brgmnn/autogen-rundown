# R7C3 - "Reckless"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** C
- **Environment:** Mines
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Establish Uplink:** Establish an uplink requiring 4 verification codes across Zones 160-171. Players begin without any ammunition or tool supplies, relying on melee combat during initial segments. The uplink process in Zone 163 spawns enemy pairs every ~30 seconds (6 total Chargers). After uplink completion, a 10:20 timer begins. Extraction from Zone 171 triggers sequential error alarms with increasing enemy waves.

### Secondary Objective

**Terminal Command:** Terminal puzzle with lights-out mechanic. Accessed via Zone 277 (requires Bulkhead Key from Zone 162). Players locate locked terminals and retrieve password information from Zones 278-279. Completing the objective permanently disables all expedition lighting and adds Shadow-type enemies to subsequent Warden Protocol waves. Must be completed before progressing further for Prisoner Efficiency (all 3 objectives).

### Overload Objective

**Dual Corrupted Uplinks:** Two sequential corrupted uplink activations. Accessed via Zone 166C with a Bulkhead Key. First uplink in Zone 329 (Blood Door spawns 4 Chargers and 6 Giants). Second uplink in Zone 331 (triggers 2 Big Chargers and 7 Chargers as ambush upon completion). Optional Zone 330 offers additional resources. Contains unfinished map areas with potential cheese spots and softlock hazards.

## Level Description

R7C3 is defined by its unique starting constraint: prisoners begin without any ammunition or tool supplies, forcing melee-only combat during the initial segments. This creates a tense early game where coordination and stealth are essential before ammunition becomes available in later zones.

The level features significant character dialogue from Klas Henriksson despite not being marked as a story-focused expedition. The main objective involves establishing an uplink with a subsequent 20-minute Warden Protocol timer that escalates with Snatcher spawns and error alarms. The secondary objective's lights-out mechanic combined with Shadow enemies fundamentally changes the remainder of the expedition.

C-Foam is heavily featured -- lockers and boxes very often contain C-Foam Grenades and C-Foam Tripmines. The level is significantly harder without human players due to the melee coordination requirements in the opening sections.

## Notable Mechanics

- **No Starting Supplies:** Players begin without ammunition or tool supplies, relying on melee
- **C-Foam Focus:** Lockers and boxes frequently contain C-Foam Grenades and C-Foam Tripmines
- **Blood Doors:** Multiple high-spawn-rate barrier doors throughout
- **Warden Protocol Timer:** 20-minute timer after main uplink with escalating enemy composition
- **Lights-Out Mechanic:** Secondary objective permanently disables all expedition lighting
- **Shadow Enemies:** Added to Warden Protocol waves after secondary completion
- **Snatcher Spawns:** Appear during the Warden Protocol escalation
- **Checkpoints:** Zones 163 and 166 serve as checkpoint locations
- **Team Scans:** Required for secondary objective completion
- **Error Alarms:** Multiple alarm types trigger during extraction and overload completion
- **Known Issues:** Unfinished map tiles in Zones 329C, 329E, 330A, 330B; invisible wall bugs; potential softlock spots; weak doors occasionally render invisible after checkpoint restarts
