# R7D2 - "AWOL"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** D
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Retrieve a Memory Stick and insert it into a Mainframe through a gauntlet of escalating enemy encounters.

1. **Zones 208-209:** Drop in and retrieve a Memory Stick from Shuttleboxes in Zone 209, defended by 5 Charger Scouts
2. **Zone 210:** Navigate through darkness with an Error Alarm spawning Strikers, Shooters, Giants, and Big Shooters; Hybrids added when the door to Zone 211 opens
3. **Zones 211-214:** Access Zone 214 via a locked terminal requiring a password from Zones 212/213
4. **Zone 212:** Contains 2 Shadow Scouts, Shadows, and Big Shadows; their presence adds Shadow types to the ongoing alarm
5. **Zone 213:** Contains 2 Charger Scouts, Chargers, and Big Chargers; adds Charger types to the alarm
6. **Zones 215-218:** Obtain a Power Cell from Zone 216 (3 Tanks + Giants and Hybrids) or Zone 217 (2 Power Cells, Big Mother, Baby Strikers, and Hybrids)
7. **Zone 218-219:** Execute command `REQUEST_MAINFRAME_ACCESS` (pauses alarm for 140 seconds)
8. **Final:** Insert Memory Stick into Mainframe; triggers final Error Alarm with Strikers, Shooters, Giants, Snatchers, and either Tank or Mother

## Level Description

R7D2 is a combat-heavy D-tier expedition defined by its unique permanent Error Alarm mechanic. A continuous, ever-modifying Error Alarm runs throughout the level, progressively adding new enemy types as players open different zones. Opening Zone 212 adds Shadows to the alarm; opening Zone 213 adds Chargers. This creates a steadily escalating threat that forces teams to balance exploration speed against resource management.

Despite being a relatively short level, R7D2 is described as one of the most resource-filled expeditions due to its heavy combat focus. Players face a strategic choice between Zone 216 (3 Tanks) and Zone 217 (Big Mother) when obtaining the Power Cell, each presenting a different boss-tier challenge.

The `REQUEST_MAINFRAME_ACCESS` command provides a critical 140-second reprieve from the alarm, giving players a window to set up before the final push. Canonically, this level demonstrates the main four characters reliably completing missions.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Permanent Evolving Error Alarm:** A continuous alarm that adds new enemy types as zones are opened
  - Zone 212 adds Shadow types
  - Zone 213 adds Charger types
- **Alarm Pause:** `REQUEST_MAINFRAME_ACCESS` pauses the alarm for 140 seconds
- **Terminal Commands:** `MAINTENANCE_UNLOCK_BRAVO` (opens door to Zone 214), `REQUEST_MAINFRAME_ACCESS` (unlocks Zone 219 and pauses alarm)
- **Boss Choice:** Zone 216 (3 Tanks) vs Zone 217 (Big Mother) -- different challenges for the Power Cell
- **Final Error Alarm:** Triggers upon Mainframe insertion with Snatchers and either Tank or Mother
- **High Resource Density:** One of the most resource-filled levels despite short length
- **5 Charger Scouts:** Zone 209 is heavily defended from the start
