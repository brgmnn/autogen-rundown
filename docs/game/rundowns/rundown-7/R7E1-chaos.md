# R7E1 - "Chaos"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 7.0: Rise
- **Tier:** E
- **Environment:** Research
- **Dimensions:** Yes (players are teleported to "Alpha Two" during mid-game progression)

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Reactor Startup:** A multi-phase reactor startup sequence -- the sole objective of this expedition.

1. **Initial Setup (Zones 442-447):** Navigate from the starting zone, collect a colored key from Zone 444, progress through locked doors and alarm zones containing Chargers and mixed enemy types. Retrieve a Power Cell from Zone 447 and insert it into a generator in Zone 442.

2. **Mid-Game Progression (Zones 443-450):** Input terminal command `COMMENCE_SCAN` to initiate a room scan, which spawns heavy Charger waves and activates Infectious Fog. Players are temporarily teleported to "Alpha Two," a separate area featuring Flesh Walls (barriers passable only by enemies but not players). Return to the main area via the `OVERRIDE_INITIALIZATION` command.

3. **Final Sequence (Zones 451-459):** Progress through respawn zones containing Mothers and Baby Strikers, then activate the reactor with a four-phase startup sequence. Each phase requires inputting codes at specific terminal locations within 1-7 minute windows, followed by enemy waves. Concludes with an Extraction Scan.

## Level Description

R7E1 is the final expedition of Rundown 7 and represents the ultimate challenge of the rundown. The level features a multi-phase reactor startup as its sole objective, taking players through over 20 zones in the Research environment.

The defining feature is the Immortal -- an invincible variant of the Tank that relentlessly pursues prisoners throughout the expedition. This enemy cannot be killed and must be avoided or kited, adding constant pressure to every encounter. The Immortal suffers from animation glitches at range that remove audio cues, making positioning difficult.

Mid-mission, players are teleported to Alpha Two, a dimensional area featuring Flesh Walls -- organic masses that block player movement but allow enemies to pass through freely. This creates asymmetrical combat scenarios where enemies can attack from positions players cannot reach.

The four-phase reactor startup features distinct enemy compositions per phase, including Flyers, Strikers, Shooters, and Tanks at different stages. Each phase has strict time limits (1-7 minutes) for code input at specific terminals.

The wiki documents extensive bugs including Scout Bombing (scouts remembering player positions through dimensional shifts), Flesh Wall clipping exploits, Immortal animation breakdowns, and Big Mother respawn failures. The expedition is designed for experienced, coordinated teams.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Immortal Enemy:** An invincible Tank variant that relentlessly pursues players throughout the entire expedition -- cannot be killed, only avoided
- **Flesh Walls:** Organic barriers that block player movement but allow enemies to pass through freely, creating asymmetrical combat
- **Dimensional Shift:** Players teleported to Alpha Two between Zones 443 and 451 during the room scan sequence
- **Infectious Fog:** Rising fog that fills zones up to torso/head height during scans and in specific zones
- **Four-Phase Reactor Startup:** Each phase has distinct enemy compositions and strict time limits (1-7 minutes) for terminal code input
- **Class V Sustain Mixed Alarm:** Present during progression
- **Respawn Zones:** Zones containing Mothers and Baby Strikers that respawn
- **Security Scans:** Room Scan, Team Scans, and Extraction Scan requirements throughout
- **Terminal Commands:** `COMMENCE_SCAN`, `OVERRIDE_INITIALIZATION`
- **Known Bugs:** Scout Bombing, Flesh Wall clipping exploits, Immortal animation breakdowns at range, Big Mother respawn failures
