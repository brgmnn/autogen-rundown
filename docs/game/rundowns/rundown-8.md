# Rundown 8.0: Duality

## Overview

- **Release Date:** December 8, 2023
- **Tiers:** A, B, C, D, E
- **Total Expeditions:** 12
- **Status:** Final rundown, concluding the game's main storyline

## Narrative Summary

The rundown is divided into two narrative halves. In the first half, prisoners aim to teleport out of an infected dimension into a clean dimension using the Matter Wave Projector and The Warden's assistance. They collect Data Cubes, encounter Nightmare enemies, and work through degausser terminals while receiving cryptic communications from an unknown team.

The second half reveals an alternate Schaeffer plotting to destroy the complex via reactor meltdown. The prisoners navigate conflicting orders, remove control chips from their biocom devices, and ultimately trick both teams to escape through a Jump Gate to an unknown fate, destroying the gate upon departure. Both original and ALT:// storylines converge in this rundown.

## New Features & Content

- **New Weapons:** Burst Pistol (Shelling Nano), Slug Shotgun (Bataldo Custom K330), Short Rifle (Drekker CLR), Melee weapon skins
- **New Enemies:** Nightmare Striker, Nightmare Shooter, Nightmare Giant, Nightmare Scout, Nightmare Babies, Zoomer Scout, Mega Mother
- **New Features:** Discord Rich Presence, Achievements system, story-driven level unlocking
- **Notable:** R8E2 is widely considered the hardest level in the game, featuring 22 of 28 total enemy types — a record for unique enemy variety in a single level
- **Much more story-focused** than all previous rundowns
