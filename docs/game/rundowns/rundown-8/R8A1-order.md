# R8A1 - "Order"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 8.0: Duality
- **Tier:** A
- **Environment:** Gardens
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Process Item:** Navigate through zones to retrieve a Matter Wave Projector and insert it into a Shuttle Box. Progress through Zone 71 (Class II Alarm, Checkpoint), Zone 72 (Class III Alarm) to retrieve a Colored Key, Zone 73 to obtain the Matter Wave Projector, then insert it into the Shuttle Box in Zone 71. Zone 74 triggers an Error Alarm (spawns 2 Strikers every ~10 seconds for the rest of the expedition). Extract from Zone 75.

### Secondary Objective

None

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker
- Shooter
- Giant
- Big Shooter

## Level Description

R8A1 is the opening expedition of Rundown 8.0, initiating the final story-arc of GTFO. The mission is more dialogue-heavy than previous rundowns. All missions through R8E1 follow the ALT Team of prisoners. This marks the ALT Team's first visit to the Gardens environment, as R7.0 events did not occur for them. The expedition serves as an introduction to the rundown's mechanics and story themes.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Class II Alarm** at Zone 71 with checkpoint
- **Class III Alarm** at Zone 72
- **Error Alarm** at Zone 74 that spawns 2 Strikers approximately every 10 seconds for the remainder of the expedition
- **Colored Key** required to unlock Zone 73
- Bio-Tracker is noted as crucial for visibility in the foliage-dense Gardens environment
- Foam and mining techniques recommended for alarm management
- Resource distribution varies significantly: Zones 70-71 offer 60-80% resources, later zones provide minimal supplies
