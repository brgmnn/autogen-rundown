# R8A2 - "Perspective"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 8.0: Duality
- **Tier:** A (functionally C/D difficulty)
- **Environment:** Standard exploration zones (Main), Nightmare Dimension / alternate Tutorial training area (Secondary)
- **Dimensions:** Yes -- The secondary objective takes place entirely in the Nightmare Dimension (Zones 70-77). The secondary sector is an alternate version of the Tutorial training area. The Nightmare Dimension is canonical but the secondary objective itself is "strictly non-canon."

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Terminal Command:** Start in Zone 50, navigate to Zone 52 and input `DRAIN_COOLING_TANKS`. Proceed to Zone 69 (Class V Mixed Alarm spawning Giants/Hybrids). Collect four decryption keys from terminals in Zones 54, 55, 56, 57, and 69. Zones 54-55 require `SMI_OVERRIDE` before accessing key terminals (triggers Room Scan with Corrupt Scans). Open Zone 68 with collected keys. Extract via Zone 67.

### Secondary Objective

**Clear a Path (Creature Study):** Input `EVAL_INIT` on a hidden terminal in Zone 69 (password-protected, changes weekly). Return to Zone 50, unlock Zone 70 via terminal command (spawns Full Team Scan). Progress through Zones 71-77 in the Nightmare Dimension. Complete Team Scans in Zone 75 and 77. Final sequence spawns 10 Nightmare enemies per ~30 seconds.

**Critical:** Choosing the Secondary Objective locks prisoners out of the Main Objective and vice versa.

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

### Main Sector

- Striker, Shooter, Giant, Hybrid, Scout
- Flyer, Shadow, Shadow Scout
- Tank (boss)

### Nightmare Dimension (Secondary)

- Nightmare Striker, Nightmare Shooter, Nightmare Scout, Nightmare Giant
- Nightmare Baby Strikers (exclusive encounter)
- Big Mother, Mother, Immortal, Zoomer Scout

## Level Description

R8A2 represents "the point of view of the OG Team" and introduces the Nightmare Dimension's existence. The secondary access is described as "impossible for the OG Team to acquire" canonically, suggesting it is an Easter egg referencing the "Nightmare Team." The mission contains zero Tool Refill Packs, and the secondary sector offers zero resources or consumables of any kind.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Mutually exclusive objectives** -- choosing Main or Secondary locks the other out
- **Zero Tool Refill Packs** in the entire mission
- **Nightmare Dimension** accessed via Zone 70 with permanent light shutdown upon entering Zone 71
- **Flesh Wall** blocks return route in the final Nightmare zone
- **Error Alarms** trigger with scaling enemy waves in the Nightmare Dimension
- **Blood Doors** that spawn Mother enemies
- **Corrupt Scans** during Room Scans in Zones 54-55
- **Password-protected terminal** (`EVAL_INIT`) with weekly changing password
- Terminal commands: `DRAIN_COOLING_TANKS`, `EXT_DECR_KEY`, `SMI_OVERRIDE`, `EVAL_INIT`, `EXEC_OVERRIDE`, `MAIN_WASTE_FLUSH`
- Full Team Scans, Room Scans, Corrupt Scans, and Colored Key security doors
