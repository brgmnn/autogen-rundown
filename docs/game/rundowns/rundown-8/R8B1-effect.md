# R8B1 - "Effect"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 8.0: Duality
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Datacenter
- **Dimensions:** Yes -- Dimensional transitions are triggered by Data Cube pickups, creating distinct zone sequences in different realities. Picking up Data Cubes teleports the team between dimensions.

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Gather Items:** Collect three Data Cubes across dimensional transitions. Navigate to Zone 138 (Class III Alarm) and retrieve the first Data Cube (triggers dimensional teleport). Proceed to Zone 139, optionally enter Zone 142 for extra resources. Enter Zone 140 (Class V Diminished Alarm) for the second Data Cube, then Zone 141 (Class V Diminished Alarm) for the third Data Cube (returns to original dimension). Pass through Zone 139 (Class IV Alarm checkpoint), collect a Bulkhead Key from Zone 140, use the key and door controller to open the Bulkhead Door to Zone 141, and extract from Zone 141.

### Secondary Objective

**Gather Items:** Use a Bulkhead Key to access Zone 172. Collect another Bulkhead Key from Zone 172. Navigate Zones 173-175 (Class V and IV Alarms). Retrieve a Data Cube from Zone 176 (teleports team back to Zone 172). Features a sleeping Tank boss in Zone 175.

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Striker, Shooter, Giant, Big Shooter, Scout
- Nightmare Striker, Nightmare Shooter (new rundown enemies introduced here)
- Zoomer Scout
- Tank (boss, in secondary)

## Level Description

R8B1 introduces the dimensional transition mechanic that becomes central to Rundown 8. Picking up Data Cubes triggers teleportation between realities, creating a non-linear zone progression. The level takes place in the Datacenter environment and serves as the first encounter with Nightmare Striker and Nightmare Shooter variants in the rundown. The secondary objective features stealth-based gameplay with an optional Tank encounter that can be avoided.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Dimensional transitions** triggered by Data Cube pickups, teleporting the team between realities
- **Multiple alarm tiers:** Class III, Class IV, and Class V Diminished Alarms
- **Bulkhead door system** for progression between zones
- **Sleeping Tank** in Zone 175 (secondary) that can be stealthed past
- Mine Deployer and C-Foam Launcher heavily recommended for alarm management
- Zone 142 offers high resource concentrations (140% Ammo, 180% Medi, 160% Tool) as an optional detour
- Foam and mining door combinations recommended for alarm management
