# R8B3 - "Cause"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 8.0: Duality
- **Tier:** B
- **Environment:** Not specified (industrial/complex zones)
- **Dimensions:** No

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Establish Uplink:** Progress from Zone 108 through a critical decision point regarding fog management. An optional detour through Zone 109 unlocks a Power Cell that opens three fog-handling doors with escalating difficulty:

- Zone 110: Collect Fog Repellers (Moderate alarm, 30 seconds)
- Zone 111: Collect Fog Turbine (Strong alarm, 100 seconds)
- Zone 112: Execute `FILTRATION_STARTUP` command (Extreme alarm, 150 seconds)

Main progression continues through Zones 114-122. Execute `LIFT_LOCKDOWN` in Zone 114 (triggers Error Alarm). Navigate through Zones 115-116, retrieve Bulkhead Key, unlock bulkhead doors to Zone 117. Navigate hostile zones 118-122 to gather passwords for the Uplink Terminal. Complete uplink in Zone 117, triggering continuous spawns of Chargers/Flyers for the remainder. Extract via Zone 108.

### Secondary Objective

**Gather Items:** Collect 10 GLP-1 Canisters distributed across four secondary zones (333-336), with three canisters per zone. Each zone presents escalating challenges including Error Alarms and active Tank spawns. Retrieve Bulkhead Key and return to main objective.

## Level Description

R8B3 is a level defined by Infectious Fog management. The level is normally filled with Infectious Fog, and players must choose from three mitigation strategies of increasing difficulty before progressing. The fog management decision creates a meaningful strategic choice that affects the entire expedition. The secondary objective involves collecting canisters across four zones with escalating threats including active Tank encounters.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Infectious Fog** fills the entire level by default, with three selectable mitigation strategies of increasing difficulty and reward
- **Fog mitigation options:** Fog Repellers (easy), Fog Turbine (medium), or Filtration Startup terminal command (hard)
- **Alarms:** Class S (Moderate/Strong/Extreme), Class IV Mixed, Error Alarms, Class III Diminished variants
- **Continuous enemy spawns** of Chargers and Flyers after completing the uplink
- **Blood doors** and respawn zones (Cocoons)
- **Bulkhead system** for progression
- Terminal commands: `LIFT_LOCKDOWN`, `FILTRATION_STARTUP`, `DEACTIVATE_ALARMS`
- Zone 117 offers maximum resources (200% Ammo, 200% Medical, 120% Tools)
- Decision-making about fog management difficulty is central to the level's design
