# R8E1 - "Valiant"

## Overview

- **Rundown:** Rundown 8.0: Duality
- **Tier:** E (highest difficulty)
- **Environment:** Mixed industrial/complex zones
- **Dimensions:** No (standard zones but Data Cube Shuttlebox mechanic)

## Objectives

### Main Objective

**Survive Warden Protocol:** A 10-step timed progression through zones 512-545 with a Warden Protocol countdown dictating zone unlocks and enemy spawns:

1. Enter Zone 512, collect Colored Key for Zone 513 security door
2. (Optional) Collect Bulkhead Key to trigger secondary objective
3. Progress to Zone 516 (Big Charger spawns)
4. Reach Zone 517, collect Colored Key for Zone 518
5. Activate stealth scan door to Zone 521
6. Progress to Zone 523, collect key for Zone 524
7. Enter Zone 525 to disable alarm
8. Enter Zone 544, insert Data Cubes into Shuttlebox (spawns Nightmare enemies)
9. Complete S Scan in Zone 545 (triggers 2-3 enemy waves)
10. Final sequence: `[//CONNECTION LOST]`

**Warden Protocol Timeline:**

- 8:30 -- Spawns Giants and Big Shooters
- 8:00 -- Unlocks Zone 515 door
- 3:30 -- Unlocks Zone 521 door
- 0:30 -- Spawns Giants and Big Shooters
- 0:00 -- Unlocks Zone 543 door

### Secondary Objective

**Terminal Command:** Accessible after collecting Bulkhead Key in Zone 512. Use Bulkhead Key with controller in Zone 530. Input command `GEN_REDIRECT_CONFIRM_Y` at terminal and return to main objective. Adding secondary increases enemy types (Shadows, Chargers, Hybrids, Flyers) and Snatcher quantity, increases alarm difficulty in Zone 544 with more Nightmare spawns, and spawns Big Charger and Snatcher.

### Overload Objective

None

## Enemy Types

- Shadow, Big Shadow
- Charger, Big Charger
- Flyer
- Giant, Big Shooter
- Snatcher
- Hybrid
- Nightmare variants (spawned in Zone 544 and during S Scan)

## Level Description

R8E1 is the first E-Tier expedition in Rundown 8.0 and represents "the first ending of the game." The mission follows the ALT Team through a timed Warden Protocol that progressively unlocks zones and spawns enemy waves on a fixed countdown. The level tests all accumulated skills with timed progression pressure. The secondary objective makes the mission significantly harder by adding more enemy types and increasing alarm difficulty. The mission ends with a cryptic `[//CONNECTION LOST]` message.

## Notable Mechanics

- **Warden Protocol countdown** with fixed timestamps that unlock doors and spawn enemies
- **Timed progression** requiring efficient navigation through zones before timer events
- **Stealth Scan (Class II)** doors incorporating stealth mechanics
- **S Scan** checkpoint in Zone 545 with spatial spread
- **Data Cube Shuttlebox** insertion in Zone 544 triggering Nightmare spawns
- **Secondary objective penalty** -- opting in significantly increases difficulty by adding enemy types, Snatchers, and Nightmare spawns
- **Security Doors** requiring Colored Keys or timer activation
- **Bioscans** required for progression
- Zone 544 has highest resources (140% Ammo, 200% Medical, 140% Tools)
- Recommended equipment: C-Foam Launchers, Bio Tracker, Lock Melters
- Terminal command: `GEN_REDIRECT_CONFIRM_Y`
